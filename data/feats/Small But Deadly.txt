Words of Power
Small But Deadly
Source
Kobolds of Golarion pg. 25
You have learned to make the most of certain weapons,
despite your physical weakness.
Prerequisites
: Weapon Focus or natural weapon, Small
size or smaller.
Benefit
:  You ignore your Strength penalty to damage
when making attacks with your racial natural weapons
and weapons for which you have the Weapon Focus feat.
