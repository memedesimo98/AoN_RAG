Words of Power
To the Last (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 217
Your devotion to battle inspires you to fight competently until death claims you.
Prerequisites
:
Diehard
,
Endurance
, worshiper of Gorum.
Benefit
:  When you are disabled, you are not staggered, though performing standard actions still deals 1 point of damage to you. Taking a full-round action deals 2 points of damage to you.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 25
You can spend 1 stamina when disabled to take a standard action without losing a hit point.
