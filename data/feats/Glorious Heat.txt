Glorious Heat
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 212
,
Faiths of Purity pg. 25
When you cast divine fire spells, their heat empowers nearby allies.
Prerequisites
: Ability to cast divine spells, caster level 5th, worshiper of Sarenrae.
Benefit
:  Whenever you cast a divine spell with the fire descriptor, choose a single ally within 30 feet that you can see. That ally heals a number of hit points equal to half your level and gains a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls until the end of its next turn.
