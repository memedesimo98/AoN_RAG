Words of Power
Groundling
Source
Advanced Player's Guide pg. 162
You can speak with burrowing animals.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, gnome, gnome magic racial trait.
Benefit
:  You can use
speak with animals
as a spell-like ability at will, but only to communicate with burrowing animals like gophers, moles, and the like. You can still use your gnome
speak with animals
ability once per day to speak with any animal, as usual.
