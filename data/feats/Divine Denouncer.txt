Words of Power
Divine Denouncer
Source
Faiths and Philosophies pg. 7
You choose to resist rather than serve the gods.
Prerequisites
:
Divine Defiance.
Benefit
:  You gain a +2 insight bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, Intimidate, Knowledge, Perform (oratory), and Sense Motive checks involving any divine spellcaster or outsider that casts spells as a cleric.
