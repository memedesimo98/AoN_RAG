Words of Power
Gunslinger
Source
Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 59
You are so skilled with firearms that you can fire one without letting down your guard.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +4,
Weapon Focus (any firearm)
, proficiency with any firearm.
Benefit
: When you attack with a firearm, you do not provoke attacks of opportunity.
Normal
: Attacking with any kind of ranged weapon in a threatened square provokes attacks of opportunity.
