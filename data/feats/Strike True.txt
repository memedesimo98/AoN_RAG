Strike True (Combat)
Source
Paths of the Righteous pg. 17
Rather than attempting a series of wild blows, you line up a perfect attack.
Prerequisites
:
Combat Expertise
, base attack bonus +6.
Benefit
: You can focus yourself as a move action. When focused, you gain a +4 bonus on your next melee attack roll before the end of your turn.
