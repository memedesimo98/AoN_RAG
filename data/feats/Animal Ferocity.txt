Animal Ferocity (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 106
When cornered and wounded, you fight like a feral beast.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +3, ferocity racial ability.
Benefit
: When your hit points are reduced below 0, you can make attacks, but you take a –5 penalty on each attack roll.
