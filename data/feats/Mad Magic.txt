Mad Magic (Combat)
Source
Advanced Class Origins pg. 27
Magic is in your blood, allowing you to cast spells no matter how furious you become.
Prerequisites
: Bloodrage class feature or perfect clarity rage power.
Benefit
:  You can cast spells from any class that grants you spells while in a bloodrage, and you keep your rage benefits when using moment of clarity during a rage. If you have the greater bloodrage class feature, you also gain a +1 bonus to the save DCs of spells you cast while in a bloodrage.
