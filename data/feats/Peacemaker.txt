Words of Power
Peacemaker
Source
Champions of Purity pg. 23
Your words of peace ring true and are much more difficult
for others to resist.
Prerequisites
: Charisma 13, good alignment.
Benefit
:  The DC to resist spells you cast to ensure
peace or force aggressive creatures to become peaceful
increases by +2. This affects spells that dissuade creatures
from aggressive actions without exerting long-term or
absolute control over them, and without leaving them
defenseless. These spells include, but are not limited to,
calm animals
,
calm emotions
,
command
,
compassionate ally
UM
,
enthrall
,
euphoric tranquility
UM
,
sanctuary
, and
serenity
UM
.
