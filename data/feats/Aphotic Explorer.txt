Aphotic Explorer
Source
Blood of the Sea pg. 9
You have trained to steel yourself against the cold when in dark, lightless realms such as the deep ocean.
Prerequisites
:
Endurance
, gillman.
Benefit
: While you are in darkness or dim light, you can take a swift action to gain cold resistance 5 for 1 round. If you have 11 or more Hit Dice, you gain cold resistance 10 instead.
