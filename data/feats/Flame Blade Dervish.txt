Words of Power
Flame Blade Dervish (Combat)
Source
Paths of the Righteous pg. 13
You move effortlessly when wielding a
flame blade
.
Prerequisites
: Ability to cast
flame blade
as a spell or spell-like ability.
Benefit
: When you cast
flame blade
, you gain a +10 enhancement bonus to your base speed as long as the spell persists, along with a +4 competence bonus on all Acrobatics checks. You add your Charisma modifier to damage rolls with your
flame blade
, and ignore the first 10 points of fire resistance possessed by a creature you hit with the
flame blade
for the purposes of determining the damage dealt by the
flame blade
. Against undead foes, you ignore the first 30 points of fire resistance. Immunity to fire still completely protects against damage from your
flame blade
.
