Stellar Wanderer
Source
Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 29
You have gained Desna’s trust, and she has taught you her realm’s secrets.
Prerequisites
: Character level 15, Knowledge (planes) 10 ranks.
Benefit
: Once per week, you can use the pools of Cynosure to instantly transport yourself from Cynosure to any plane, as per
plane shift
.
