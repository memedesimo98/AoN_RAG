Storm Warrior
Source
Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 70
Your weapons channel the fury of thunderstorms.
Prerequisites
:
Storm Soul
, cloud or storm giant.
Benefit
:  You can transfer the power of storms from your body to any metal melee weapons you wield, dealing an additional 1d6 points of electricity damage.
