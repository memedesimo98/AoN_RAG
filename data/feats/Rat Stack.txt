Rat Stack (Combat, Teamwork)
Source
Blood of the Beast pg. 23
You coordinate efficiently in tight spaces.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13, swarming racial trait.
Benefit
: You can occupy the same square with up to two other creatures that also have this feat. If all participants also have the Tunnel Rat
ARG
feat, up to four creatures can share the same space.
