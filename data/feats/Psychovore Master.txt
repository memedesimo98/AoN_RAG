Psychovore Master (Combat)
Source
Pathfinder: Spiral of Bones #3 pg. 29
When your unarmed attacks strike true. you also damage your target's mind and gain power from it.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Unarmed Strike
,
Psychovore Strike
,
Psychovore Style
, Sense Motives 15 ranks.
Benefit
: When you score a critical hit against the creature you are gaining a dodge bonus to AC against from Psychovore Style with your unarmed strike, that target also takes 1 point of Intelligence and Wisdom damage and you gain 2d8 temporary hit points. These temporary hit points last for 1 hour and don't stack with other temporary hit points you might gain with this feat.
