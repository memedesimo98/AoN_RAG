Words of Power
Renown
Source
Pathfinder Society Primer pg. 9
Word of your pre-Pathfinder accomplishments precedes
you, and you know how to make the most of your
previous reputation.
Benefit
:  When in a settlement of 5,000 people or more,
treat your Fame score as 2 higher for the purposes of
qualifying for Prestige Awards or purchasing equipment.
This feat doesn’t grant you any additional Prestige Points.
Normal
:  Your Fame score is determined by the total
number of Prestige Points earned over the course of
your career.
