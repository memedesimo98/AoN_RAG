Words of Power
Lone Wolf
Source
Rise of the Runelords Player's Guide pg. 15
Although you may have grown up in the city, you led a lonely childhood and were forced to fend for yourself.
Prerequisites
: 1st-level character, cannot have City Born or Country Born.
Benefit
: Whenever you are dying, your chance of stabilizing is 50%. Your vigorous health also grants you a +1 bonus on Fortitude saves.
