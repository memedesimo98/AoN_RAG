Extra Panache (Panache)
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 147
You have more panache than the ordinary swashbuckler.
Prerequisites
: Amateur Swashbuckler or panache class feature.
Benefit
:  You gain two more panache points at the start of each day, and your maximum panache increases by two.
Special
: If you have levels in the swashbuckler class, you can take this feat multiple times. Its effects stack.
