Improved Learn Ranger Trap
Source
Kobolds of Golarion pg. 24
Your skill with traps is extensive.
Prerequisites
:
Learn Ranger Trap
, Survival 8 ranks.
Benefit
:  You learn one additional ranger trap and can
now use both extraordinary and supernatural versions of
any ranger traps you know.
