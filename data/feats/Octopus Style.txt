Octopus Style (Combat, Style)
Source
Martial Arts Handbook pg. 15
You anticipate the attacks of more than one enemy at once.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13,
Dodge
,
Improved Unarmed Strike
, Acrobatics 1 rank, Perception 1 rank.
Benefit
: As a move action when using this style, you can select a number of creatures you are aware of equal to your Dexterity modifier. You can’t be flanked by the selected creatures until the start of your next turn.
