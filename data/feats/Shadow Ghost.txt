Words of Power
Shadow Ghost
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 112
You can move between the Shadow Plane and the Material Plane more often.
Prerequisites
: Fetchling,
shadow walk
spell-like ability.
Benefit
:  You gain the ability to use
shadow walk
an additional time each day.
Special
:  You may select this feat multiple times. Its effects stack.
