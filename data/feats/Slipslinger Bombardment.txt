Slipslinger Bombardment (Combat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 13
You can sling a rain of splash weapons at your enemies.
Prerequisites
:
Quick Draw
,
Slipslinger Grenadier
,
Slipslinger Style
,
Throw Anything
,
Weapon Focus (sling) or weapon training (thrown) class feature
, warslinger racial trait
ARG
.
Benefit
:  You can use a sling to hurl alchemical splash weapons as ammunition with Slipslinger Grenadier during an attack action or a full-attack action, loading your sling with such weapons as a free action.
Special
:  This feat can’t be used with alchemists’ bombs.
