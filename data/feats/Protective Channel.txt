Protective Channel
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 215
When you channel energy, Iomedae grants protection against dark forces.
Prerequisites
: Channel energy 7d6, worshiper of Iomedae.
Benefit
:  When you channel positive energy to heal living creatures, you can choose to affect healed creatures with a
protection from evil
spell (caster level equal to your effective cleric level).
