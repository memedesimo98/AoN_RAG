Extra Light and Dark
Source
Blood of Shadows pg. 11
You can twist the effects of positive and negative energy for longer periods of time.
Prerequisites
: Light and dark racial trait, wayang.
Benefit
:  Your light and dark ability lasts 1 additional minute per day.
Special
:  You can take this feat multiple times. Its effects stack.
