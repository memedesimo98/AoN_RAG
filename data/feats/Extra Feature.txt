Extra Feature
Source
Blood of the Moon pg. 7
You are an exceptional shapechanger.
Prerequisites
: Con 13, skinwalker.
Benefit
:  When you change shape to your bestial form, you may choose one additional feature from those listed in your shapechange ability and gain that benefit while in bestial form.
Special
:  You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects stack.
