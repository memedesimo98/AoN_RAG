Masterful Flourish (Combat, Performance)
Source
Inner Sea Combat pg. 12
Your training with gladiatorial weapons gives you the upper hand when using them in performance combat.
Prerequisites
: Weapon training class feature.
Benefit
:  When wielding a weapon with the performance weapon special quality that belongs to a category of weapons in which you have weapon training, you may add the bonus from your weapon training to any performance combat checks made as a result of using the weapon.
