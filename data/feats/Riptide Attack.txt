Riptide Attack (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 215
,
Faiths of Balance pg. 24
You can immediately drag opponents you successfully trip.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Drag
APG
,
Improved Trip
, worshiper of Gozreh.
Benefit
:  Whenever you succeed at a trip combat maneuver, you can automatically attempt a drag combat maneuver against your tripped target as a swift action.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 25
By spending 4 stamina points, you can attempt a drag
APG
combat maneuver check against any opponent that you successfully trip using Riptide Attack as a free action instead of a swift action.
