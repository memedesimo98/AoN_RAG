Deadly Troupe (Teamwork)
Source
Inner Sea Races pg. 204
You combine elements of dance and acting with your combat style to distract your enemies.
Prerequisites
: Perform (act) or Perform (dance) 3 ranks, human (Varisian).
Benefit
:  When at least one ally who also has this feat is threatening a foe, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus against that foe on Bluff checks to feint, Bluff checks to create a diversion to hide, and Acrobatics checks to move through that foe’s threatened squares. You also gain a +2 circumstance bonus on steal and dirty trick combat maneuver checks made against that foe.
