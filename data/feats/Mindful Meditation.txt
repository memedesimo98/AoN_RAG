Mindful Meditation (Meditation)
Source
Blood of the Beast pg. 28
You clear your mind of stress and emotion by focusing on calming breathing exercises.
Prerequisites
: Wis 11,
Meditation Master.
Benefit
: For 24 hours after you meditate, you gain a +1 bonus on Will saving throws against charm, compulsion, emotion, and fear effects, and increase the Bluff DC to feint you and the Intimidate DC to demoralize you by +5. These bonuses increase by 1 for every 5 Hit Dice you have to a maximum increase of +4 at 20 Hit Dice.
