Hammer Guards the Anvil (Teamwork)
Source
Elemental Master's Handbook pg. 7
Your allies cover you when your guard is down.
Benefit
: Creatures threatened by an ally with this feat cannot make attacks of opportunity against you when you pick up an item from the ground, retrieve a stored item, sheathe a weapon, or stand up from prone.
