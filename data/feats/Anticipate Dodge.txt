Anticipate Dodge (Combat)
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 141
Your knowledge of mobility and your attack prowess allow you to thwart elusive opponents.
Prerequisites
:
Dodge
, Mobility; base attack bonus +7, brawler level 4th, or monk level 4th.
Benefit
:  You automatically know whether a creature you can see has a dodge bonus to its AC. You gain up to a +2 bonus on attack rolls against a target that has a dodge bonus. This bonus cannot exceed the dodge bonus of the creature you attack.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 113
You can spend 1 stamina point to increase the maximum bonus from Anticipate Dodge by 2 (this is still limited by the creature’s dodge bonus, however).
