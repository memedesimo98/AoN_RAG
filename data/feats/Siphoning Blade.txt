Siphoning Blade (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 59
You can empower yourself by drawing blood through your rhoka sword.
Prerequisites
:
Voracious Blade
,
Weapon Focus (rhoka sword)
, base attack bonus +8, urdefhan.
Benefit
:  When you use the Voracious Blade feat to deal Strength damage with your rhoka sword, you also drain the victim’s blood. The attack deals 1 point of bleed damage in addition to its normal damage, and you gain fast healing 2 for 3 rounds. You can use this ability only once against an individual creature in a 24-hour period.
