Hinderance Dismissal
Source
Magic Tactics Toolbox pg. 13
You’ve mastered the art of dismissing certain spells when they become a problem for you or your allies.
Benefit
: You can dismiss any pit spell (such as
create pit
APG
,
hungry pit
APG
,
spiked pit
APG
, and similar spells with “pit” in their names) or wall spell (such as
wall of fire, wall of ice
, and similar spells with “wall” in their names) you cast. The spells must have a duration of at least 1 round. This ability follows the normal rules for dismissing spells, allowing you to dismiss conjured pits and walls as a standard action.
Normal
: Several pit and wall spells cannot be dismissed without the use of this feat.
