Treant's Call
Source
Wilderness Origins pg. 11
You have learned some words of the mystic language treants use to call trees to their defense.
Prerequisites
: Cha 17, ability to cast
speak with plants
, character level 13th,
gathlain
.
Benefit
: You can cast
liveoak
once per day as a spell-like ability, but its duration is only 10 minutes.
