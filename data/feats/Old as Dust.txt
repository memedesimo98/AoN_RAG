Words of Power
Old as Dust
Source
Monster Codex pg. 80
You have been dead for so long you have become quite difficult to kill.
Prerequisites
:
Sleeper
,
Warren Digger
, creature has been a ghoul for at least 500 years.
Benefit
:  When you are reduced to 0 hit points, you are not destroyed—you cannot be killed unless your body is completely destroyed with fire or doused in holy water, or you take damage from a cure spell or channeled positive energy after you’ve been reduced to 0 hit points. Anytime you are reduced to 0 hit points, you remain unconscious until you are healed or until the following midnight, at which time you regain 1 hit point per Hit Die and awaken.
