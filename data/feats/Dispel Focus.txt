Dispel Focus
Source
Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 9
You are skilled at the art of dispelling.
Prerequisites
: Ability to cast
dispel magic
.
Benefit
:  Whenever you attempt a dispel check based on your caster level, you gain a +2 bonus on the check.
