Tree Leaper
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 119
You use the flexibility of tree branches to aid in making arboreal leaps.
Benefit
: When you are in a tree, you count as having a running start when you jump.
Normal
: You must have at least 10 feet of space for a running start.
