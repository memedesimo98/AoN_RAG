Special Delivery (Teamwork)
Source
Distant Shores pg. 63
Your partner can carry your spells to their targets.
Prerequisites
:
Bonded Mind
,
Share Spells
, ability to deliver spells through an animal companion, eidolon, familiar, or phantom, caster level 3rd.
Benefit
:  Whenever you are in contact with an ally who has the Bonded Mind feat and you cast a touch spell, you can designate the ally as the “toucher,” as per the deliver touch spells familiar ability.
