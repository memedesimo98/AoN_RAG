Spiritualist's Call
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 140
Whenever you summon your phantom, it is more powerful for a brief period of time.
Prerequisites
: Phantom class feature.
Benefit
:  Whenever you summon your phantom, you can give it a +2 enhancement bonus to its Strength, Dexterity, or Charisma. This bonus lasts for 10 minutes after the summoning ritual is complete.
