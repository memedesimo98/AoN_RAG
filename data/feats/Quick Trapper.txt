Quick Trapper
Source
Pathfinder #116: Fangs of War pg. 67
Your trap-making proficiency improves greatly.
Prerequisites
: Learn Ranger Trap or trap class feature, Survival 5 ranks.
Benefit
:  When setting a ranger trap (
Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic
64), you can do so as a standard action rather than a full-round action. If you do so, the trap DC is reduced by 5.
