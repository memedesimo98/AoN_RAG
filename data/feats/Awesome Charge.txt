Awesome Charge (Combat)
Source
Monster Codex pg. 68
When you charge, you can send your opponent flying.
Prerequisites
: Str 25,
Awesome Blow
,
Improved Bull Rush
,
Power Attack.
Benefit
:  When you hit your opponent with a charge attack, you can attempt an awesome blow combat maneuver against that opponent as a free action.
