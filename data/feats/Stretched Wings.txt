Stretched Wings
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 201
You strengthen your crippled wings.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Skill Focus (Fly)
, strix, wing-clipped racial trait.
Benefit
:  Your strix racial fly speed increases to 60 feet (average). You ignore the wing-clipped trait’s Fly check requirement to fly upward.
