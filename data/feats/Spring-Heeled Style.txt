Spring-Heeled Style (Armor Style, Combat, Style)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 11
You cunningly dart across the battlefield from foe to foe, taking advantage of your armor’s flexibility to duck blows.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13,
Dodge
,
Mobility
, Shot on the Run or Spring Attack, base attack bonus +4, proficiency with light armor.
Benefit
: If using this style during any round in which you take a move action to move 10 or more feet, you gain a +2 bonus on weapon attack rolls until the start of your next turn. While using this style, you gain the AC bonus from Mobility against attacks of opportunity provoked by making a ranged attack or loading a ranged weapon.
Special
: A character with the armor training class feature can use Spring-Heeled Style while wearing any type of armor with which she is proficient.
