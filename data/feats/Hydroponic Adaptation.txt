Hydroponic Adaptation
Source
Legacy of the First World pg. 8
You can change your wings to instead be effective underwater.
Prerequisites
: Gathlain, racial fly speed.
Benefit
: Once per day as a full-round action, you can lose your fly speed to gain a swim speed of 30 feet as you reconfigure your symbiotic vines to help you move underwater. This effect lasts for 1 hour, or until you end it as a full-round action.
