Parting Blast
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 137
When you die, your body explodes in one final, destructive kinetic blast.
Prerequisites
: Kinetic blast class feature.
Benefit
:  You can accept 1 point of burn to prepare a kinetic blast that automatically triggers upon your death. If you are killed at any point before your burn is removed, your body instantly erupts in an explosion that deals an amount of damage equal to that of your simple blast to all creatures in a 5-foot radius. A parting blast destroys your body, which might prevent any magic that requires an intact corpse.
