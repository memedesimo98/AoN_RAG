Fearsome Finish (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 212
,
Faiths of Corruption pg. 24
When you slay enemies, you frighten other opponents.
Prerequisites
:
Intimidating Prowess
, worshiper of Lamashtu.
Benefit
:  Upon reducing an enemy to 0 or fewer hit points, for the remaining duration of the combat encounter, you gain a +4 bonus on any Intimidate checks made to demoralize opponents who can see both you and the opponent you dropped.
