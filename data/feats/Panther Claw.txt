Words of Power
Panther Claw (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 112
You unleash a rapid series of blows on foes that attempt to attack you when you move.
Prerequisites
: Wis 15,
Combat Reflexes
,
Improved Unarmed Strike
,
Panther Style.
Benefit
:  While using Panther Style, you can spend a free action, instead of spending a swift action, to make a retaliatory unarmed strike. You can make a number of retaliatory unarmed strikes on your turn equal to your Wisdom modifier.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 128
When making a retaliatory strike, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
