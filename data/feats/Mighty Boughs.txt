Mighty Boughs
Source
Wilderness Origins pg. 10
Your wings are particularly strong and sprout sharp barbs.
Prerequisites
: Str 15, character level 11th,
gathlain
.
Benefit
: You gain two wing attacks. These are secondary natural attacks that deal 1d3 points of slashing damage (or 1d4 if you are Medium).
