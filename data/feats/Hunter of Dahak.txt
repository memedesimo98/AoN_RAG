Hunter of Dahak
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 213
You viciously hunt and sacrifice metallic dragons.
Prerequisites
: Favored enemy (dragon), worshiper of Dahak.
Benefit
:  Your favored enemy bonuses increase by 2 against metallic dragons.
