Intimidate Animals (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 115
You are skilled at intimidating animals with your bluster and fury.
Prerequisites
:
Greater Wild Empathy
, Intimidate 5 ranks, Knowledge (nature) 5 ranks, wild empathy class feature.
Benefit
: You add your Wisdom modifier on Intimidate checks against animals in addition to your Charisma modifier. An animal with 4 or fewer Hit Dice is frightened instead of shaken on a successful Intimidate check to demoralize it.
