Climbing Vine
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 24
You can climb like a vine.
Prerequisites
: Str 13, vine leshy.
Benefit
:  You gain a climb speed of 10 feet. If you already have a natural climb speed, your climb speed increases by 10 feet. You can take this feat multiple times, but your climb speed can never exceed your base speed.
