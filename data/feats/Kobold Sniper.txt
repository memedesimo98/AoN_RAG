Words of Power
Kobold Sniper (Combat)
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 137
You snipe quickly, making it harder for others to find the location of your attack.
Prerequisites
: Stealth 1 rank, kobold.
Benefit
:  When you are sniping, you only take a –10 penalty on your Stealth checks to stay hidden.
Normal
:  You take a –20 penalty on your Stealth check to maintain your obscured location while sniping.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125
When you are sniping, you can spend 5 stamina points to decrease the penalty on Stealth checks to stay hidden to –5 until the start of your next turn.
