Words of Power
Deny Death
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 148
Your
ki
is so strong that it can deny death.
Prerequisites
:
Ki
pool,
Endurance.
Benefit
:  As long as you have 1
ki
point in your
ki
pool, when you fail your Constitution check to stabilize, you do not lose 1 hit point. If you succeed at the check, you can spend 1
ki
point to heal 1d6 hit points. If you roll a natural 20 on the check to stabilize, you can spend 1
ki
point to heal 2d6 hit points of damage instead.
