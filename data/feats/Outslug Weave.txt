Outslug Weave (Combat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 16
You land precise attacks from afar.
Prerequisites
: Int 13; Combat Expertise or brawler’s cunning
ACG
class feature;
Lunge
;
Outslug Style
; Weapon Focus with the chosen weapon.
Benefit
:  While using Outslug Style, you don’t take a –2 penalty to your AC when using the Lunge feat. The bonuses to AC and on weapon damage rolls that you gain from Outslug Style increase to +2.
