Words of Power
Intrusive Presence
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 135
You can rifle through the thoughts and memories of your host.
Prerequisites
: Int or Cha 21,
Hidden Presence
,
Manipulative Presence
, ability to cast
possession
or
greater possession
.
Benefit
:  You can use
detect thoughts
at will as a spell-like ability against a host creature you possess. The host receives no saving throw, and the effect functions as if you had concentrated for 3 rounds. You can also use
seek thoughts
on your host as a spell-like ability once during the possession. The host can attempt a Will save with a DC equal to the DC of the original possession effect. If the host succeeds, the effect fails and the host become aware of the possession if it wasn’t already.
