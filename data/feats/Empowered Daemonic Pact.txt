Empowered Daemonic Pact
Source
Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 59
Your violent death brings woe to the enemies of your daemon masters.
Prerequisites
: Urdefhan.
Benefit
:  When you succeed at using your daemonic pact ability and allow your soul to be consumed, the resulting blast’s radius increases to 10 feet, and you gain a +2 profane bonus to the DC of the Reflex save victims must attempt to halve this damage.
Normal
:  A successful use of the daemonic pact ability deals negative energy damage to creatures in a 5-foot radius.
