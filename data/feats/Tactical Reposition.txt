Tactical Reposition (Combat)
Source
Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 8
You excel at moving foes right where you want them.
Prerequisites
: Int 13,
Combat Expertise
,
Improved Reposition
APG
.
Benefit
:  When making a reposition combat maneuver (
Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player’s Guide
322), you can move an enemy into a trap or other hazardous area, such as a pit, wall scythe, or
blade barrier
. When you do so, the moved enemy is treated as though it had activated the trap or triggered the hazard, and it takes a –2 penalty to AC and on saving throws to mitigate the trap or hazard’s effects.
Normal
:  You cannot reposition a foe into an intrinsically dangerous space such as a pit or
blade barrier
.
