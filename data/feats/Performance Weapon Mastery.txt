Performance Weapon Mastery (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 112
You wield all your weapons with the flair of a performer.
Benefit
:  You treat all weapons you are proficient in as if they had the performance weapon quality (page 144).
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 128
As long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool, you treat all weapons—not just the ones you are proficient with—as if they had the performance
UC
weapon quality.
