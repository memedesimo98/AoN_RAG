Words of Power
Psychic Maestro
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 138
You can use two occult skill unlocks more often.
Prerequisites
: Psychic Sensitivity or the ability to cast psychic spells.
Benefit
:  Choose two occult skill unlocks. You can use each of those one additional time per day (or per week, in the case of automatic writing). You must have at least 1 rank in the skills associated with the chosen occult skill unlocks.
