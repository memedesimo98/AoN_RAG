Pesh Euphoria
Source
Black Markets pg. 18
,
Dark Markets - A Guide to Katapesh pg. 56
The calming effects of pesh make you difficult to disturb.
Prerequisites
: Sahir-Afiyun or pesh addict.
Benefit
:  When you take a dose of pesh, in addition to its normal initial effect, you gain a +2 bonus on saves against emotion
UM
and fear effects. Once per day, if you fail a saving throw against an emotion
UM
or fear effect while you are under the initial effect of pesh, you can attempt another saving throw 1 round later at the same DC.
