Words of Power
City Sprinter
Source
Ultimate Intrigue pg. 80
You are skilled at moving quickly through forbidding parts of cities.
Prerequisites
:
Street Smarts.
Benefit
: You do not treat crowds as difficult terrain. You also gain an additional +2 bonus on Acrobatics checks to move along rooftops and on slippery sections of city streets and sewers.
