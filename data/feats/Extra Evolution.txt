Extra Evolution
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 150
Your eidolon has more evolutions.
Prerequisites
: Eidolon class feature.
Benefit
:  Your eidolon’s evolution pool increases by 1.
Special
:  This evolution can be taken once at 1st level, and again at 5th, 10th, 15th and 20th.
