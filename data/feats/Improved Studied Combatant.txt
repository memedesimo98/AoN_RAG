Improved Studied Combatant
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 150
Deep understanding of your foes’ tactics grants you the upper hand in combat.
Prerequisites
: Int 13,
Amateur Investigator
,
Studied Combatant
, base attack bonus +8, 1 rank in at least one Knowledge skill, no levels in a class that has the inspiration class feature.
Benefit
:  Your bonuses for Studied Combatant increase to a +4 insight bonus on melee attack rolls and a +4 bonus on damage rolls. This feat otherwise works like the Studied Combatant feat.
