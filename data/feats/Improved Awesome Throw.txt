Improved Awesome Throw (Combat)
Source
Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 69
When you perform an awesome throw, you knock opponents back farther.
Prerequisites
: Str 25,
Awesome Throw
,
Greater Bull Rush
, Throw Anything or rock throwing, size Large or larger.
Benefit
:  When you make an awesome throw combat maneuver, for every 5 by which your combat maneuver check exceeds the target’s CMD, you send the target flying 5 additional feet. Furthermore, this movement provokes attacks of opportunity from your allies.
