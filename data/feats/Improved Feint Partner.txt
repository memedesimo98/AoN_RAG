Words of Power
Improved Feint Partner (Combat, Teamwork)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 105
Knowledge of your companions’ tricks and techniques allow you to take even greater advantage of your allies’ feints.
Prerequisites
: Bluff 1 rank,
Combat Reflexes
,
Feint Partner
, base attack bonus +6.
Benefit
:  Whenever an ally who also has this feat successfully feints against an opponent, that opponent provokes an attack of opportunity from you.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 123
When you feint, you can spend 5 stamina points for each additional ally to grant an attack of opportunity to any number of additional allies—not just those with this feat.
