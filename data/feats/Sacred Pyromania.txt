Sacred Pyromania
Source
Heroes of Golarion pg. 10
Your faith in
Sarenrae
and the power of flame allows you to lace fire with holy power and burn things that normally can’t be burned.
Prerequisites
:
Goblin
, worshipper of
Sarenrae
.
Benefit
: Whenever you make an attack or cast a spell that deals fire damage, the fire damage applies to incorporeal creatures, regardless of whether the fire is magical. An incorporeal creature’s 50% reduction in damage from corporeal sources does not apply to any fire damage that you deal.
