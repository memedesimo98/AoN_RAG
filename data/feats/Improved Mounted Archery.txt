Improved Mounted Archery
Source
Adventurer's Guide pg. 15
You are an expert at making ranged attacks while mounted.
Prerequisites
:
Mounted Archery
,
Mounted Combat
, Ride 1 rank.
Benefit
: You take no penalty when using a ranged weapon while mounted and taking a double move action. The penalty while your mount is running is reduced to –2.
