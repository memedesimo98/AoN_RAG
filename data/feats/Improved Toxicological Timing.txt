Improved Toxicological Timing
Source
Alchemy Manual pg. 8
You know how to adjust the amount of time required for a poison to take effect.
Prerequisites
: Craft (alchemy) 11 ranks,
Toxicological Timing.
Benefit
:  Whenever you craft a poison with the Craft (alchemy) skill, you can add or subtract up to 1 day from its onset time (to a minimum onset period of 1 round).
