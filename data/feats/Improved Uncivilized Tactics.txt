Improved Uncivilized Tactics (Combat)
Source
Heroes of the Wild pg. 23
You are skilled at twisting your foe’s limbs out of joint.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Power Attack
,
Uncivilized Tactics.
Benefit
:  You don’t provoke an attack of opportunity when attempting a combat maneuver check to use Uncivilized Tactics. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on such checks.
Normal
:  You provoke an attack of opportunity when using the Uncivilized Tactics feat.
