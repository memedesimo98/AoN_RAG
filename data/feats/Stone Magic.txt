Words of Power
Stone Magic
Source
Giants Revisited pg. 49
,
Pathfinder #4: Fortress of the Stone Giants pg. 57
Knowledgeable even among stone giant elders, you have unlocked greater mastery over earth and stone.
Prerequisites
: Cha 17, stone giant elder (age 700 years or older).
Benefit
:  In addition to the usual spell-like abilities of a stone giant elder, you gain the ability to use
passwall
,
spike stones
,
statue
,
stoneskin
, and
wall of stone
once per day as spell-like abilities (caster level 10th, the save DCs are Charisma-based).
