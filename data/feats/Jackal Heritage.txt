Jackal Heritage
Source
Rival Guide pg. 41
A jackalwere crouches somewhere in your family tree.
Prerequisites
: Humanoid, must be taken at 1st level.
Benefit
: You gain a +2 racial bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting effects, and a +2 racial bonus on Perception checks.
