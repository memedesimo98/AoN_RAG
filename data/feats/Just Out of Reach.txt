Just Out of Reach (Combat)
Source
Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 9
The farther an attacker is from you, the more easily you avoid its melee attacks.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13,
Dodge
,
Mobility.
Benefit
:  When a foe more than 5 feet away from you makes a melee attack against you using reach, you gain a +4 dodge bonus to your AC against the attack.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 21
You can spend 5 stamina points to gain the benefits of this feat against any one melee attack. (
Melee Tactics Toolbox 9
)
