Bred Commander
Source
Monster Codex pg. 116
You possess a long pedigree of ruthless generals and military tacticians, and an army under your command is more difficult to rout as a result.
Prerequisites
: Hobgoblin.
Benefit
:  When serving as a commander using the mass combat rules presented in Chapter 4 of
Ultimate Campaign
, you can add your class level to your army’s Morale checks in place of your Charisma modifier.
