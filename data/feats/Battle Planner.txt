Battle Planner
Source
Chronicle of Legends pg. 8
Your study of heroes and combat has made you exceptionally suited for directing allies to their most advantageous positions.
Prerequisites
:
Inspire action class feature.
Benefit
: When using the inspire action ability to grant an extra move action, you can affect all allies within 30 feet.
