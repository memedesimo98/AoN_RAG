Words of Power
Stone Soul
Source
Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 70
You’re so attuned to the earth that your skin can harden to the thickness of stone.
Prerequisites
: Stone giant.
Benefit
:  Your natural armor bonus increases by 1. Once per day, you can use
stoneskin
(on yourself only) as a spell-like ability. Your caster level is equal to 1/2 your Hit Dice.
Special
:  You can take this feat up to three times. Each additional time you take this feat, the number of times you can use stoneskin per day increases by one.
