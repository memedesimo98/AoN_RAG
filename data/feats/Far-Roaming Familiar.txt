Far-Roaming Familiar
Source
Familiar Folio pg. 18
Distance can’t sever the bond between you and your familiar.
Prerequisites
: Intelligence 13, must have a familiar.
Benefit
:  You retain your empathic link to your familiar regardless of distance, though lead blocks the link at distances greater than 1 mile (similar to the way lead blocks
detect magic
effects).
