Believable Veils
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 207
The teachings of your deity help you keep your secrets safe.
Prerequisites
: Caster level 3rd, ability to cast at least one glamer or figment, worshiper of Sivanah.
Benefit
:  Once per day when you create a glamer or figment effect, you can empower the effect so that the DC to disbelieve it is 2 higher than normal.
