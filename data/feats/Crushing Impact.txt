Crushing Impact (Combat)
Source
Martial Arts Handbook pg. 24
You slam into your foes with brute strength as unyielding as a wall.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Improved Bull Rush
,
Improved Unarmed Strike
,
Power Attack
.
Benefit
: Whenever you successfully bull rush an enemy and its movement is stopped by a solid object or barrier, you deal your unarmed strike damage to the foe, as long as it’s within your threatened area. If you performed your bull rush as part of a charge, you gain a +2 bonus on your damage roll.
