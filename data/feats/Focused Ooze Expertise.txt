Focused Ooze Expertise (Combat)
Source
Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 23
You’ve learned to avoid and overcome the engulfing attacks of your studied prey.
Prerequisites
: Favored enemy (ooze) class feature.
Benefit
: You gain a bonus to your CMD equal to half your favored enemy (ooze) bonus. You also gain acid resistance equal to half your favored enemy (ooze) bonus. Against oozes, you instead apply your full favored enemy (ooze) bonus for both benefits.
