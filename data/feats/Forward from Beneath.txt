Forward from Beneath (Story)
Source
Pathfinder #131: The Reaper's Right Hand pg. 78
You have vowed to infiltrate your wicked kin and redeem your family’s reputation.
Prerequisites
: Be a member of House Kastner, be a good-aligned member of a family or organization whose secret evil has been publicly revealed, or have the Dishonored Family or Betrayal backgrounds.
Benefit
: Your experience with infiltrating your own family or organization gives you a +2 bonus on Disguise checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in Disguise, this bonus increases to +4. If you are not evil, as a standard action you can choose to radiate a faint aura of evil for the purposes of spells such as
detect evil
. You can dismiss this ersatz aura as a standard action.
Goal
: Redeem your family or organization.
Completion Benefit
: You gain the benefit of
undetectable alignment
as a constant spell-like ability. You can suppress or resume this ability as a standard action.
