Greater Channel Force
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 88
Your eruptions of divine power move your enemies.
Prerequisites
:
Channel Force
,
Improved Channel Force
, aasimar, channel energy 6d6.
Benefit
:  When using Improved Channel Force, you can affect all creatures in a 30-foot-radius burst.
