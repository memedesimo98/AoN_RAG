Words of Power
Survivor
Source
Inner Sea World Guide pg. 289
,
Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 129
Only the strong thrive in your homeland, and you are no weakling, even among your kin.
Prerequisites
: Con 13,
Diehard
,
Endurance.
Benefit
: You gain a +5 bonus on all Constitution checks made to stabilize while dying. Once per day, if you are struck by a critical hit or sneak attack, you can spend an immediate action to negate the critical or the sneak attack damage, making the attack a normal hit.
