Distance Thrower (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 97
You are accurate with thrown weapons at longer ranges than normal.
Prerequisites
: Str 13.
Benefit
:  With a thrown weapon, you reduce your penalty on ranged attack rolls due to range by 2.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 118
When making an attack with a thrown weapon, you can spend 2 stamina points to reduce your penalty on ranged attack rolls due to range by an additional 4.
Mythic Distance Thrower
Source
Mythic Adventures pg. 63
There’s virtually no limit to your powerful throwing arm.
Prerequisites
: Str 17,
Distance Thrower
UC
.
Benefit
:  Thrown weapons wielded by you have twice their normal range.
