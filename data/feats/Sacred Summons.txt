Sacred Summons
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 155
The minions of your divine patrons stand ready to answer your call.
Prerequisites
: Aura class feature, ability to cast
summon monster
.
Benefit
:  When using
summon monster
to summon creatures whose alignment subtype or subtypes exactly match your aura, you may cast the spell as a standard action instead of with a casting time of 1 round.
