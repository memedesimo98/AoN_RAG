Improved Possession
Source
Demons Revisited pg. 32
Your ability to possess bodies is more powerful.
Prerequisites
: Demonic Possession or shadow demon, Cha 25
Benefit
:  The duration of your
magic jar
spell-like ability increases to 24 hours. You can choose to activate a host body’s extraordinary abilities, supernatural abilities, spelllike abilities, and spells known or prepared.
