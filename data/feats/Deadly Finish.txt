Words of Power
Deadly Finish (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 94
Your attacks don’t just fell your opponents—they kill them outright.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +11.
Benefit
:  When you hit with a melee attack and reduce your opponent to –1 or fewer hit points, you can force that opponent to succeed at a Fortitude save (DC 15 + the damage your attack dealt) or die.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 116
When you reduce an opponent to 0 hit points, you can spend 2 stamina points to force the creature to succeed at the saving throw for Deadly Finish or die.
