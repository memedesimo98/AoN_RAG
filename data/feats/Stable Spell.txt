Stable Spell (Metamagic)
Source
Wardens of the Reborn Forge pg. 49
You can cast spells in areas of primal magic with less chance of triggering a primal magic event.
Benefit
:  In areas of primal magic, the chance of a stable spell triggering a primal magic event is reduced by 25%. In cases where a primal magic event would trigger despite the reduced chances, the caster of a stable spell receives a +2 bonus on the concentration check to avoid the primal magic event. A stable spell uses up a spell slot 1 level higher than the spell’s actual level.
