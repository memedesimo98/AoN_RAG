Kyton Shield (Combat)
Source
Horror Adventures pg. 88
Whether in your hand or draped around your body, the spiked chain serves as both weapon and shield.
Prerequisites
: Wis 15,
Exotic Weapon Proficiency (spiked chain)
,
Kyton Style
, base attack bonus +5, Knowledge (planes) 3 ranks, ki pool class feature.
Benefit
: When using Kyton Style, you gain a +1 shield bonus to your Armor Class as long as you are wielding a spiked chain. If the spiked chain is magical, add 1/2 its enhancement bonus to this shield bonus. If the spiked chain has the vicious ability, instead add its full enhancement bonus to the shield bonus.
