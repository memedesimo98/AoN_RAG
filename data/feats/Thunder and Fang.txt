Thunder and Fang (Combat)
Source
Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 10
,
Pathfinder #10: A History of Ashes pg. 28
You have mastered the ancient Shoanti Thunder and Fang fighting style, allowing you to fight with increased effectiveness when wielding an earth breaker and klar.
Prerequisites
: Str 15,
Two-Weapon Fighting
,
Weapon Focus (earth breaker)
,
Weapon Focus (klar)
Benefit
:  You can use an earth breaker as though it were a one-handed weapon. When using an earth breaker in one hand and a klar in your off hand, you retain the shield bonus your klar grants to your Armor Class even when you use it to attack. Treat your klar as a light weapon for the purposes of determining your two-weapon fighting penalty.
Normal
:  An earth breaker is a two-handed weapon, preventing the use of a klar in one hand without imposing penalties for using the earth breaker one-handed. A klar can be used either as a one-handed weapon or a shield; it does not grant a bonus to AC during rounds in which it is used as a weapon.
Special
:  This is a combat feat, and may be selected with class features that grant bonus combat feats.
