Words of Power
Lunge (Combat)
Source
PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 130
You can strike foes that would normally be out of reach.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +6.
Benefit
: You can increase the reach of your melee attacks by 5 feet until the end of your turn by taking a –2 penalty to your AC until your next turn. You must decide to use this ability before any attacks are made.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125
When you use this feat, you can spend 2 stamina points to avoid taking the –2 penalty to AC for that use.
Mythic Lunge
Source
Mythic Adventures pg. 69
Your reaching strikes aid your defenses against those you hit.
Prerequisites
:
Lunge.
Benefit
:  Whenever you use Lunge and hit a creature with the melee attack, you no longer take a –2 penalty to AC against that creature. You can expend one use of mythic power when you use Lunge to negate the –2 penalty to AC whether you hit or miss, and gain a +2 bonus on attacks of opportunity you make while Lunge is in effect.
