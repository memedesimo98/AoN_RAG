Words of Power
Fox Shape
Source
Dragon Empires Primer pg. 5
You can change into a fox in addition to your other forms.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, base attack bonus +3, kitsune.
Benefit
:  You can take the form of a fox (
Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 3
112) whose appearance is static and cannot be changed each time you assume this form. Your bite attack’s damage is reduced to 1d3 points of damage on a hit, but you gain a +10 racial bonus on Disguise checks made to appear as a fox. Changing from kitsune to fox shape is a standard action. This ability otherwise functions as
beast shape II
, and your ability scores change accordingly.
