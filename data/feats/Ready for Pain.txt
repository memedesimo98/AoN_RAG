Ready for Pain
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 138
Your implanted trick hardens the body against damage.
Prerequisites
: Mesmerist trick class feature.
Benefit
:  When you implant a trick, you can grant the subject DR 1/— against nonlethal damage until the trick is triggered or its duration ends. This DR increases by 1 for every 5 mesmerist levels you possess.
