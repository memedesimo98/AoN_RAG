Words of Power
Bat Shape
Source
Blood of the Moon pg. 9
Your powers of transformation have been honed to the point where you can wholly become a bat.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, werebat-kin.
Benefit
:  You can take the form of a bat whose appearance is static and cannot be changed each time you assume this form. You gain a +10 racial bonus on Disguise checks to appear as a bat. Changing from werebat-kin to bat shape is a standard action. This ability otherwise functions as
beast shape II
, and your ability scores change accordingly.
If you wear a
bat pelt
(see page 31), you may choose to adopt a form resembling the bat whose skin you wear instead of your normal bat form.
