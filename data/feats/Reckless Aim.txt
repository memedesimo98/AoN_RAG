Reckless Aim (Combat)
Source
Blood of Fiends pg. 25
Your lack of regard for others proves a boon when you fire projectiles into melee.
Prerequisites
:
Point-Blank Shot
,
Precise Shot.
Benefit
:  When you shoot or throw ranged weapons at an opponent engaged in melee, you can choose to take a –1 penalty to your AC and gain a +2 competence bonus on your attack roll. However, when you roll a natural 1 on a ranged attack roll made with this bonus, you automatically hit a random adjacent creature that threatens your intended target.
