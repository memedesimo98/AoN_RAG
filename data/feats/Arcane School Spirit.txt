Arcane School Spirit
Source
Gnomes of Golarion pg. 30
You can talk up the power of your own school of magic to such an extent that creatures find it more difficult to resist your spells.
Prerequisites
: Bluff 1 rank, arcane school class feature, gnome.
Benefit
: As a full-round action, you extol the virtues of your school of magic to one creature within 30 feet. Make a Bluff check opposed by a target’s Sense Motive check; if you win the check, the target takes a –2 penalty on saving throws made to resist the next spell of this school that you cast. If your chosen school is universalist, choose a specific school each time you use this ability. If your Bluff check succeeds, however, your lack of true commitment to the school means the target takes only a –1 penalty on its saving throw.
