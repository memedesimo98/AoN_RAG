Words of Power
Animal Affinity
Source
PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 118
You are skilled at working with animals and mounts.
Benefit
: You get a +2 bonus on all Handle Animal and Ride skill checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in one of these skills, the bonus increases to +4 for that skill.
Mythic Animal Affinity
Source
Mythic Adventures pg. 55
You have a greater affinity with wild animals, and can speak with them.
Prerequisites
:
Animal Affinity.
Benefit
:  The bonus on Handle Animal and Ride skill checks from Animal Affinity increases by 2. In addition, you can expend one use of mythic power to
speak with animals
as the spell. Your caster level for this effect is equal to twice your tier.
