Wind Rider
Source
Blood of the Ancients pg. 27
You have trained yourself to move and fight in strong winds, allowing you to use their elemental power to boost your own speed.
Prerequisites
: Acrobatics or Fly 3 ranks.
Benefit
: When flying within strong winds, you gain a +15-foot bonus to your fly speed. For every wind strength category above strong, you gain an additional +15-foot bonus to your fly speed (for a total of +30 feet in severe winds, +45 feet in a windstorm, and +60 feet in tornadoes). This feat does not give you any bonuses on Fly or Strength checks to determine if you are potentially checked or blown away.
