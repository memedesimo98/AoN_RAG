Street Carnage (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Intrigue pg. 92
For you, the urban environment becomes another brutal weapon you can use to shred flesh and break bones.
Prerequisites
: Str 15,
Improved Bull Rush
,
Improved Unarmed Strike
,
Power Attack
,
Street Style
,
Street Sweep
, base attack bonus +8 or monk level 7th.
Benefit
: While using Street Style, the critical multiplier of your unarmed strikes becomes ×3, instead of ×2.
