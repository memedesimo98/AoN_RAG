Curious Companion
Source
Wilderness Origins pg. 21
Heroes tend to pick up the oddest friends—birds of the sky and beasts of the wood. Your companions are strange even by these standards.
Prerequisites
: Animal companion class feature.
Benefit
: You can select a plant companion or vermin companion as your animal companion.
