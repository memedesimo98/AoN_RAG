Words of Power
Strangler (UC) (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 121
Throttling the life out of enemies is second nature to you.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13, sneak attack +1d6,
Improved Grapple
,
Improved Unarmed Strike.
Benefit
:  Whenever you successfully maintain a grapple and choose to deal damage, you can spend a swift action to deal your sneak attack damage to the creature you are grappling.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133
As long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool, you can automatically deal your sneak attack damage to a creature you are grappling when you successfully maintain the grapple, instead of taking a swift action to deal damage.
