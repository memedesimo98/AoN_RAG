Words of Power
Feint Defender (Combat, Shield Mastery)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 18
You use your shield to conceal your own body language and keep your attention focused.
Prerequisites
:
Shield Focus
, base attack bonus +6 or fighter level 4th, Sense Motive 1 rank.
Benefit
: You add your shield’s bonus to AC to the DC of feint attempts against you.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 20
You can spend 5 stamina points to force a foe to attempt two Bluff checks when trying to feint against you in combat, and use the lower of the two results. (See page 18.)
