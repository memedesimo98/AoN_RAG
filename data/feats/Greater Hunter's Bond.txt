Greater Hunter's Bond (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 113
Your allies can target additional foes.
Prerequisites
: Improved Hunter’s Bond, hunter’s bond (hunting companions) class feature, ranger level 12th.
Benefit
: When you activate hunter’s bond, you can select a number of targets equal to your Wisdom modifier as long as they all count as your favored enemy.
Normal
: You select one target against which your allies receive your favored enemy bonus.
