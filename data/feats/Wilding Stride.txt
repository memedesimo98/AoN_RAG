Wilding Stride
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 120
Your wild-hearted drive and honed physique allow you to move with great swiftness.
Prerequisites
: Con 13,
Wilding.
Benefit
: Your base speed increases by 10 feet. This does not stack with the fast movement class feature or similar effects, unless that class feature or effect provides an enhancement bonus.
