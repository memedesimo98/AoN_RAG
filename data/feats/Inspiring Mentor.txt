Inspiring Mentor
Source
Ultimate Intrigue pg. 85
Others listen to your words of encouragement and perform acts above and beyond as a result.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, inspire competence bardic performance.
Benefit
: Inspire competence now affects all allies within 30 feet who can hear your performance, as long as they are attempting the skill you’ve selected.
Normal
: Inspire competence only affects one ally.
