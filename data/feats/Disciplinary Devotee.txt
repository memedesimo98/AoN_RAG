Disciplinary Devotee
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 129
Your careful study of mental magic grants you mastery in your discipline.
Prerequisites
: Psychic discipline class feature.
Benefit
:  You gain a +1 bonus on caster level checks and concentration checks when casting spells from your psychic discipline, and you gain a +1 insight bonus on saving throws against spells from your discipline.
