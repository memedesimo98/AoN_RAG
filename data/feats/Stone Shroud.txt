Stone Shroud
Source
Heroes from the Fringe pg. 9
Your connection to the earth allows you to find the perfect spot to hide among the stone.
Prerequisites
: Wis 13, Stealth 1 rank, dwarf, stonecunning racial trait.
Benefit
: You gain a +4 bonus on Stealth checks while in areas of rocky terrain or while underground.
