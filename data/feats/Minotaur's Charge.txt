Minotaur's Charge
Source
Classic Monsters Revisited pg. 43
Your charge sends foes reeling away, knocking them to the ground.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Improved Bull Rush
, powerful charge.
Benefit
: When you hit an opponent with a charge attack, you might also initiate a bull rush as a free action. This does not require you to move into your opponent’s square and does not provoke an attack of opportunity. If you successfully push your opponent 15 feet or more, he is also knocked prone.
