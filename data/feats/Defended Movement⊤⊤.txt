Defended Movement (Combat, Shield Mastery)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 18
You deftly block attacks with your shield while moving through battle.
Prerequisites
:
Shield Focus
, base attack bonus +6 or fighter level 4th.
Benefit
: You gain a +2 bonus to your AC against attacks of opportunity.
Special
: Defended Movement counts as both Dodge and Mobility for the purposes of feats that have Dodge and Mobility as prerequisites.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 20
You can spend 3 stamina points as an immediate action to increase the bonus to your AC to +4 against a single attack. (See page 18.)
