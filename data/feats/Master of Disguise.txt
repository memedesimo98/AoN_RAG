Master of Disguise
Source
Pathfinder #22: The End of Eternity pg. 75
You are particularly adept at impersonating a specific being.
Prerequisites
: Cha 12,
Skill Focus (Disguise) 5 ranks.
Benefit
: Choose an individual. You gain a +4 on all Disguise checks made to impersonate that specific creature. You can choose an additional individual for every 4 levels you possess.
