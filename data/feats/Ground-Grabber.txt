Ground-Grabber (Combat)
Source
Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 23
When pressed by big opponents, you hunker down to more easily keep your feet.
Prerequisites
: Con 13.
Benefit
:  You gain a +2 competence bonus to your Combat Maneuver Defense against awesome blow, bull rush, drag, reposition, or trip combat maneuvers from opponents larger than you.
