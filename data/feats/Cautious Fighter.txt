Cautious Fighter (Combat)
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 66
You care more about survival than victory.
Prerequisites
: Halfling.
Benefit
:  When fighting defensively or using total defense, your dodge bonus to AC increases by 2.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 115
When not fighting defensively or using total defense, you can spend 5 stamina points to increase your dodge bonus by 2. This increase lasts until the start of your next turn.
