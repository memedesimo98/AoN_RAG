Voice of Beasts
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 119
Your deep connection with nature allows you to speak with all manner of living creatures.
Prerequisites
: Wild shape class feature.
Benefit
: While you are using wild shape, you gain the ability to communicate with all animals. This acts as speak with animals, though the effect is supernatural in nature and can’t be dispelled.
