Grappled Caster
Source
Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 15
You have trained to cast spells while wrestling an opponent.
Prerequisites
:
Combat Casting.
Benefit
:  You gain a +4 bonus on concentration checks to cast spells while grappled. This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by Combat Casting.
