Relentless Cheer (Teamwork)
Source
Inner Sea Races pg. 208
Your undying optimism keeps you and your companions going even when things get rough.
Prerequisites
: Halfling, fearless and halfling luck racial traits.
Benefit
:  Adjacent allies who also have this feat gain a +1 morale bonus on all saving throws. This bonus increases to +3 on saving throws against fear.
