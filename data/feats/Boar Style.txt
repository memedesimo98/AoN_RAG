Boar Style (Combat, Style)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 90
Your sharp teeth and nails rip your foes open.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Unarmed Strike
, Intimidate 3 ranks.
Benefit
:  You can deal bludgeoning damage or slashing damage with your unarmed strikes—changing damage type is a free action. While using this style, once per round when you hit a single foe with two or more unarmed strikes, you can tear flesh. When you do, you deal 2d6 extra points of damage with the attack.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 114
While using this style, you can spend 3 stamina points when you hit a target with only one unarmed strike during a round to tear flesh and deal 2d6 points of damage with the attack. You can still only tear flesh once per round.
