Improved Stellar Wanderer
Source
Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 29
You have mastered the art of planar travel through your time on Cynosure.
Prerequisites
:
Stellar Wanderer
, Knowledge (planes) 15 ranks.
Benefit
: When using the
Stellar Wanderer
feat for planar travel, you can choose up to eight creatures to be transported with you. You now appear 1 to 100 miles (1d%) from your intended destination rather than 5 to 500 miles.
