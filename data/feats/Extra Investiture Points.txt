Extra Investiture Points
Source
Heroes of the High Court pg. 23
Whatever power has invested you to guide your destiny has instilled you with additional energy.
Prerequisites
: Investiture class feature.
Benefit
: Your investiture points increase by 2.
Special
: You can take Extra Investiture Points multiple times. The effects stack.
