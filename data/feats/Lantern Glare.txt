Lantern Glare (Combat)
Source
Distant Realms pg. 33
Like an archon’s aura, the light of your thrown weapons can shake even the fiercest of foes.
Prerequisites
:
Lantern Light
,
Lantern Style
,
Point-Blank Shot
,
Precise Shot
, good or lawful alignment, caster level 11th or monk level 11th.
Benefit
: Creatures you hit with a thrown weapon as well as creatures within 20 feet of your target must succeed at a Will save (DC = 10 + half your character level + your Charisma modifier) or become shaken until they damage you or 24 hours have passed. A creature that succeeds at its saving throw or that damages you after becoming shaken becomes immune to this effect for 24 hours.
