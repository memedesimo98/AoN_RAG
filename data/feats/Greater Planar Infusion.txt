Greater Planar Infusion (Conduit)
Source
Planar Adventures pg. 28
You have mastered the powers of another plane.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Planar Infusion
,
Planar Infusion
, character level 13th.
Benefit
: Choose a plane for which you’ve gained an improved infusion via Improved Planar Infusion. You gain that plane’s greater infusion as well.
