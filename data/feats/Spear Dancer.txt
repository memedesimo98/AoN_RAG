Words of Power
Spear Dancer (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 216
,
Faiths of Purity pg. 25
You incorporate your two-handed reach weapon into a dizzying martial dance.
Prerequisites
:
Weapon Focus (any two-handed reach weapon)
, Perform (dance) 4 ranks.
Benefit
:  Each time you hit a creature with a twohanded reach weapon that you have Weapon Focus in, the creature is dazzled for 1 round.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 25
When you inflict the dazzled condition on an opponent using the Spear Dancer feat, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to increase the duration of the effect by 1 round per stamina point spent.
