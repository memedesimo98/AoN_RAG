Elf-Magic Defense
Source
Pathfinder #115: Trail of the Hunted pg. 74
You can better resist arcane trickery from your hated foes.
Prerequisites
: Favored enemy class feature, hobgoblin.
Benefit
:  In addition to the normal benefits against your favored enemies, you add half your favored enemy bonus on saving throws made against arcane spells, spell-like abilities, and supernatural abilities of your favored enemies.
