Fury's Fall (Combat)
Source
Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 26
You can use strength and agility to send foes crashing to the ground.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Trip.
Benefit
: When making a trip attack, add your Dexterity bonus to your CMB.
