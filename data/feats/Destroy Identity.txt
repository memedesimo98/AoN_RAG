Words of Power
Destroy Identity (Combat, Critical)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 210
,
Faiths of Corruption pg. 24
You can use your weapons to mar your opponents’ most distinctive characteristics.
Prerequisites
:
Critical Focus
, base attack bonus +11, worshiper of Lamashtu.
Benefit
:  Whenever you confirm a critical hit against an opponent, in addition to dealing critical damage as normal, you maim your target so much that it takes 2 points of Charisma damage and becomes staggered for 1 round.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24
When you confirm a critical hit against an opponent, you can spend 4 stamina points to increase the Charisma damage that opponent takes from Destroy Identity to 1d3+1 points.
