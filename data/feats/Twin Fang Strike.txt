Twin Fang Strike (Combat)
Source
Villain Codex pg. 105
You strike at your enemies with both weapons simultaneously.
Prerequisites
: Dex 15;
Quick Draw
;
Twin Fang Style
; Two- Weapon Fighting; Acrobatics 6 ranks; base attack bonus +6 or monk level 6th.
Benefit
: While using Twin Fang Style, when you take an attack action while wielding two daggers or two kama, you can strike with both your primary and off-hand weapons. If you do so, both attacks take a –4 penalty and you apply precision damage and effects that occur when you hit only once, even if you hit with both attacks.
