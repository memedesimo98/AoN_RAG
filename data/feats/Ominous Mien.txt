Ominous Mien (Faction)
Source
Faction Guide pg. 57
Your walk, stance, and voice all amplify your status as a Hellknight when you cast spells or use spell-like abilities against opponents.
Prerequisites
: Hellknight rank, Hellknight 10 TPA.
Benefit
: Whenever you cast a spell or use a spell-like ability from the compulsion, fear, phantasm, or shadow sub-schools, add +1 to the DC of the saving throw against that spell or effect.
