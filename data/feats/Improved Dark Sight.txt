Improved Dark Sight
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 112
With further modification, your eyes see clearly in not only normal darkness, but in magical darkness as well.
Prerequisites
:
Dark Sight
,
Gloom Sight
, fetchling.
Benefit
:  You gain the see in darkness ability and lose the light sensitivity weakness, but gain the light blindness weakness.
