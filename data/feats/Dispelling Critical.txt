Dispelling Critical (Critical)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 97
Your blows attack the physical and arcane forms of your enemies at the same time.
Prerequisites
:
Arcane Strike
, base attack bonus +11, ability to cast
dispel magic
.
Benefit
:  If you have
dispel magic
prepared or can cast it spontaneously, when you score a critical hit against an opponent, you may use a swift action to cast
dispel magic
to make a targeted dispel against that opponent.
