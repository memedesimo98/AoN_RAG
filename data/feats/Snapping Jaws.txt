Snapping Jaws (Combat)
Source
Monster Codex pg. 93
When enemies get too close, your jaws tear flesh and bone.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +1, gnoll.
Benefit
:  You can use your bite as a natural weapon. This is a primary attack that deals 1d6 points of damage.
