Find the Flaw
Source
Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 15
You sense the minute imperfections in the otherwise ordered structure of your foes’ weapons and armor.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Improved Sunder
,
Power Attack
, base attack bonus +1, Craft (armor or weapons) 5 ranks, aphorite.
Benefit
: When performing a sunder combat maneuver, you ignore a number of points of hardness equal to your Intelligence modifier.
