Exsanguinate (Combat)
Source
Horror Adventures pg. 87
You drink blood from the living at a furious rate, often draining creatures dry in a matter of seconds.
Prerequisites
: Blood drain universal monster ability, grab universal monster ability.
Benefit
: Once per round, when you successfully pin an opponent, that opponent takes double the normal damage from your blood drain.
