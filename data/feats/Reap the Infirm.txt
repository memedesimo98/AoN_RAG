Reap the Infirm (Combat)
Source
Villain Codex pg. 68
You can use a creature’s diseased state against it.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +3.
Benefit
: When you hit a diseased creature with a weapon attack, you deal an additional 1d6 points of damage. This is precision damage. Additionally, you gain a +2 bonus on combat maneuver checks against diseased creatures.
