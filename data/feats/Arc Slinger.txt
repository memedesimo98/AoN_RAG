Words of Power
Arc Slinger (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 89
You can twirl your sling in a way that maximizes its effectiveness.
Prerequisites
:
Point-Blank Shot
, proficient with sling or halfling sling staff.
Benefit
:  When using a sling or sling staff, you reduce your penalty on ranged attack rolls due to range by 2. Point-Blank Shot’s damage bonus applies within the first normal range increment of your sling (50 feet) or sling staff (80 feet).
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 113
You can spend 1 stamina point when making a sling or sling staff attack to gain the benefits of Point-Blank Shot within the first two range increments of the sling or staff.
