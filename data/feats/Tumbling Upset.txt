Tumbling Upset (Combat)
Source
Martial Arts Handbook pg. 25
You nimbly dodge beneath your opponent, creating an opportunity to upend your foe.
Prerequisites
: Int 13,
Combat Expertise
,
Improved Trip
, Acrobatics 7 ranks.
Benefit
: When you succeed at an Acrobatics check to move through an enemy’s space, you can spend a swift action to attempt a trip combat maneuver check against the opponent. Even if you fail this check by 10 or more, you do not fall prone.
Normal
: If you fail a trip combat maneuver check by 10 or more, you are knocked prone.
