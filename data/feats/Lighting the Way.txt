Lighting the Way (Story)
Source
Pathfinder #131: The Reaper's Right Hand pg. 79
You seek to lead others through your pious example.
Prerequisites
: Be a member of House Zespire, or have the Devoted, Exemplar, Marked by the Gods, or Righteous Mentor background.
Benefit
: Once per day as a standard action, you can give all allies within 30 feet who can hear you a +1 bonus on attack rolls and on saving throws against mind-affecting effects for 1 minute.
Goal
: Establish your religion in an existing settlement where it isn’t present. You can do this by having a settlement gain the
Holy Site or Pious quality
for devotion to your deity, or change its government to a theocracy based on your religion.
Completion Benefit
: You can spontaneously convert any 2nd-level or higher divine spell into
enthrall
and can spontaneously convert any 3rd-level or higher divine spell into
suggestion
.
