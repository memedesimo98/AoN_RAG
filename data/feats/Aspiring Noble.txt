Aspiring Noble (Story)
Source
Heroes of the High Court pg. 4
You strive to be seen as a legitimate noble.
Prerequisites
: Must be a member of an established family that is yet unrecognized as nobility.
Benefit
: You have learned to further your name among the people. Choose a settlement and either Diplomacy or Intimidate. While in your chosen settlement, you gain a +2 bonus on checks with the chosen skill when dealing with the commoners of that settlement. If you have 10 or more ranks in the chosen skill, the bonus increases to +4.
Goal
: Have your family recognized as nobility by either three established noble families or a ruling monarch.
Completion Benefit
: You gain a +2 bonus on checks with your chosen skill when dealing with the rest of the citizens of your chosen settlement. Your bonus to interact with commoners increases to +4, or it increases to +6 if you have 10 or more ranks in your chosen skill.
