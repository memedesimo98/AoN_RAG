Words of Power
Rapid Grappler (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 114
You are a quick hand at grappling.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13,
Greater Grapple
,
Improved Grapple
,
Improved Unarmed Strike
, base attack bonus +9 or monk level 9th.
Benefit
:  Whenever you use Greater Grapple to successfully maintain a grapple as a move action, you can then spend a swift action to make a grapple combat maneuver check at a -5 penalty.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130
You can spend 2 stamina points to gain a +2 bonus on a grapple check made as a swift action with this feat.
