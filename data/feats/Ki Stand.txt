Ki Stand
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 153
If an opponent knocks you down, you swiftly rebound with an attack.
Prerequisites
:
Ki
pool.
Benefit
:  While you have at least 1
ki
point in your
ki
pool, you can stand up as a swift action that provokes attacks of opportunity.
You may spend 1 point of your
ki
pool to stand up as a swift action without provoking attacks of opportunity.
