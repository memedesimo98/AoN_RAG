New Thassilonian Magic
Source
Paths of the Righteous pg. 23
You use Thassilonian magic in a nontraditional way that preserves the ancient traditions while lifting some of its restrictions.
Prerequisites
: Thassilonian specialization (
Pathfinder Campaign Setting: Inner Sea Magic 17
).
Benefit
: You choose one of your two opposition schools. You also gain Thassilonian as a bonus language. If you already speak Thassilonian, you gain one of the following languages as a bonus language instead: Aklo, Azlanti, Giant, Shoanti, or Varisian.
Normal
: A Thassilonian specialist cannot customize his choice for opposition schools.
