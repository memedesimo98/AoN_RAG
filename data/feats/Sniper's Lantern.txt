Sniper's Lantern
Source
Monster Codex pg. 32
You use your innate magic powers to target enemies and spot their weaknesses.
Prerequisites
:
Point-Blank Shot
,
dancing lights
racial spell-like ability.
Benefit
:  You can use your
dancing lights
racial spell-like ability to create one orb of penetrating light. This light grants you a +2 insight bonus on all ranged attacks made against targets within 5 feet of the light’s location. You can direct this light to a new location anywhere within the spell’s range as a move action, otherwise the effect functions exactly like
dancing lights
. You can use this ability once per day, plus an additional time per day when your caster level for your racial
dancing lights
spell-like ability reaches 5th, 10th, 15th, and 20th.
