Aldori Style (Combat, Style)
Source
Adventurer's Guide pg. 24
You have learned the classic Aldori dueling stance.
Prerequisites
:
Alertness
,
Exotic Weapon Proficiency (Aldori dueling sword)
,
Weapon Finesse
,
Weapon Focus (Aldori dueling sword).
Benefit
: While using Aldori Style and wielding only a single Aldori dueling sword in one hand (not using a shield, an off-hand weapon, armor spikes, unarmed strikes, or natural weapons), when you fight defensively or use Combat Expertise, you gain a +2 bonus on weapon damage rolls.
