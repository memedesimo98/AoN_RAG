Protective Line (Combat, Teamwork)
Source
Villain Codex pg. 56
Your teammates look out for you while you reload.
Prerequisites
:
Exotic Weapon Proficiency (firearms)
UC
.
Benefit
: When you are adjacent to an ally who also has this feat, you do not provoke attacks of opportunity while loading a firearm.
