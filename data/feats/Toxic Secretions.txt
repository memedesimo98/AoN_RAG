Toxic Secretions
Source
Potions and Poisons pg. 4
Your unusual diet makes you produce more poison than others of your kind.
Prerequisites
: Grippli, toxic skin alternate racial trait.
Benefit
: You gain two additional uses of your toxic skin alternate racial trait per day. This feat can be taken multiple times.
Normal
: The grippli toxic skin alternate racial trait can be used once per day.
