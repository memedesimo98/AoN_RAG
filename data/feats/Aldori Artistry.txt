Aldori Artistry (Combat)
Source
Adventurer's Guide pg. 24
You can do more with your blade than slash and stab.
Prerequisites
:
Exotic Weapon Proficiency (Aldori dueling sword)
,
Weapon Finesse
, base attack bonus +2.
Benefit
: Choose one of the following combat maneuvers: disarm, reposition
APG
, steal
APG
, sunder, or trip. You gain a +2 bonus when performing the selected maneuver while wielding an Aldori dueling sword. This bonus does not stack with the bonus provided by a combat maneuver feat with “improved” in its name (such as Improved Trip).
Special
: You can select this feat multiple times, choosing a different combat maneuver each time.
