Manifold Stare (Combat, Stare)
Source
Magic Tactics Toolbox pg. 9
Your hypnotic gaze causes tremendous pain in its victims.
Prerequisites
: Mesmerist level 3rd, painful stare
OA
class feature.
Benefit
: You can trigger your painful stare one additional time per round.
Special
: This feat can be taken once by a 3rd-level mesmerist, and again when he reaches 9th and 15th levels. Its effects stack. A mesmerist can take this feat in place of a bold stare improvement
OA
