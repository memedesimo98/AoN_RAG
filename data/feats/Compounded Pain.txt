Compounded Pain (Combat)
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 129
Your painful stare causes two additional effects.
Prerequisites
: Mesmerist level 13th, painful stare class feature.
Benefit
:  You can apply the effects of two stare feats to your painful stares.
Normal
:  You can apply the effects of only one stare feat to a given painful stare.
