Words of Power
Shrewd Tactician (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea World Guide pg. 289
Your dealings with pirates, thieves, and assassins have taught you to be exceedingly careful.
Prerequisites
:
Alertness
,
Combat Reflexes.
Benefit
: Opponents do not gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls for flanking you, although they can still sneak attack you. You also gain a +3 bonus on Sense Motive checks made to resist a foe’s Bluff checks to feint in combat.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 25
When an opponent attempts a feint against you, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to increase the DC of the skill check made to feint by 1 per point of stamina spent.
