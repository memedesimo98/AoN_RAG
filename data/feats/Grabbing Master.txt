Grabbing Master (Combat)
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 148
You can grapple two foes as easily as one.
Prerequisites
:
Grabbing Drag
,
Grabbing Style
, Improved Grapple; base attack bonus +12, brawler level 8th, or monk level 8th.
Benefit
:  When you are grabbing two opponents while using Grabbing Style, you can use your grapple to move or damage one or both opponents you are grappling, instead of just one.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120
When you are grappling only one opponent while using Grabbing Style
ACG
, you can spend 5 stamina points to continue to threaten the area within your reach with your free hand until the beginning of your next turn.
