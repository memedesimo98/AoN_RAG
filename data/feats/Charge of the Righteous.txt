Words of Power
Charge of the Righteous (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 209
,
Faiths of Purity pg. 24
You charge toward undead and fiends with confidence in your righteous cause.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +1, any good alignment.
Benefit
:  You do not take a penalty to AC while charging undead creatures and evil outsiders.
Normal
:  When you charge, you take a –2 penalty to AC until the start of your next turn.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24
When charging any creature with an evil alignment, you can spend 2 stamina points in order to ignore the –2 penalty to AC imposed by charging.
