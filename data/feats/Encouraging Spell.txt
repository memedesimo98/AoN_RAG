Encouraging Spell (Metamagic)
Source
Magic Tactics Toolbox pg. 5
Your inspiration provides your allies with greater support.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, Diplomacy 6 ranks.
Benefit
: Any morale bonus granted by an encouraging spell is increased by 1. An encouraging spell uses up a spell slot 1 level higher than the spell’s actual level.
