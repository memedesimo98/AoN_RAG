Focused Fey Expertise (Combat)
Source
Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 23
Your attacks strike true against capricious targets like fey.
Prerequisites
: Favored enemy (fey) class feature.
Benefit
: You gain a bonus on attack rolls to confirm critical hits equal to half your favored enemy (fey) bonus. You treat your weapon attacks against fey as cold iron for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction and regeneration.
