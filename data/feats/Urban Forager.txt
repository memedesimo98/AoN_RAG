Words of Power
Urban Forager
Source
Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 29
You are skilled at keeping yourself and others safe in the alleys, sewers, and underbellies of cities.
Prerequisites
: Knowledge (local) 4 ranks, Survival 4 ranks, Track.
Benefit
: You may use the Survival skill to forage and track in urban locations using the table below.
DC
Task
10
Get along in an urban setting, and easily find enough food and supplies to survive for 24 hours. You can provide food and water for one other person for every 2 points by which your check result exceeds 10.
15
Gain a +2 bonus on all Constitution checks made to resist starvation or thirst. You may grant the same bonus to one other character for every 1 point by which your Survival check exceeds 15.
15
Gain a +2 bonus on all Survival checks made to track on firm or hard ground.
