Words of Power
Dragonheart
Source
Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 25
Your familiarity with dragonkind makes you less
susceptible to their abilities.
Prerequisites
: Knowledge (arcana) 1 rank.
Benefit
:  You gain a +1 bonus on all saving throws
against auras, breath weapons, spell-like abilities, spells,
supernatural abilities, and other special attacks of
creatures with the dragon type.
