Vengeful Death Vow
Source
Pathfinder: Worldscape #1 pg. 28
Your death vow strikes terror into the hearts of your enemies, making them less likely to hit you in combat.
Prerequisites
: Death vow class feature.
Benefit
: Targets of your death vow take a -2 penalty on attacks made against you.
