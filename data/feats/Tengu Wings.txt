Tengu Wings
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 165
You can grow wings that allow you to fly.
Prerequisites
: Character level 5th, tengu.
Benefit
:  Once per day, you can sprout a pair of giant black crow’s wings, granting you a fly speed of 30 feet (average maneuverability). This spell-like ability otherwise functions as
beast shape I
(though you do not gain any other benefits of that spell) with a caster level equal to your level.
