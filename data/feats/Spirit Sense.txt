Spirit Sense
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 140
You sense the presence of the unseen.
Prerequisites
: Psychic Sensitivity or ability to cast psychic spells.
Benefit
:  You gain a +5 bonus on checks to notice haunts. In addition, when you target an incorporeal creature with a corporeal spell or effect that doesn’t deal damage, you have a 75% chance of affecting the target.
Normal
:  Corporeal spells and effects that don’t deal damage have only a 50% chance of affecting an incorporeal creature.
