Spring-Heeled Sprint (Armor Style, Combat)
Source
Armor Master's Handbook pg. 11
You can move quickly without sacrificing your offense.
Prerequisites
: Dex 15,
Dodge
,
Mobility
, Shot on the Run or Spring Attack,
Spring-Heeled Style
, base attack bonus +7, proficiency with light armor.
Benefit
: While using Spring-Heeled Style, you can move up to twice your speed when using the Shot on the Run or Spring Attack feats
