Giantslaying Team (Combat, Teamwork)
Source
Inner Sea Races pg. 206
When a giant attempts to hit your ally, that giant unwittingly reveals an opening in her defenses.
Prerequisites
: Dex 13,
Dodge
,
Mobility
, base attack bonus +4, defensive training racial trait.
Benefit
:  Whenever a foe with the giant subtype you threaten misses with an attack of opportunity against an ally with this feat, you can make a melee attack against that giant as an immediate action.
