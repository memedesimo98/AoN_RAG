Longshanks
Source
Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 70
Your long stride allows you extra maneuverability.
Prerequisites
: Giant subtype, size Large or larger.
Benefit
:  Your land speed increases by 5 feet, and you can ignore up to 20 feet of nonmagical difficult terrain each round you move.
