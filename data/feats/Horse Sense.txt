Horse Sense
Source
Pathfinder #132: The Six-Legend Soul pg. 73
You take Grand Prince Beldam I’s insistence upon the resilience of simple creatures to heart, emulating them to shield yourself from complex mental effects.
Prerequisites
: Wis 15,
Iron Will
.
Benefit
: When you attempt a saving throw against an enchantment effect that cannot affect an animal (such as
charm person
), you can roll the saving throw twice and take the better result.
