Merciless Rush (Combat)
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 214
,
Faiths of Corruption pg. 25
Though hordes stand against you, they rarely stand long.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Bull Rush
, worshiper of Rovagug.
Benefit
:  When you bull rush a creature and your check exceeds the target’s CMD by 5 or more, you deal damage equal to your Strength modifier to that target.
