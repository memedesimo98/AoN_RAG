Champion of Tranquility (Alignment)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 99
The harmony of law is your highest ideal.
Prerequisites
: 10 Hit Dice, lawful neutral alignment.
Benefit
: You can store a number of
affirmations
up to your Charisma bonus (minimum 1) to use at any time, not just within the next 24 hours. You can use an affirmation to cast
calm emotions
as a spell-like ability as a standard action, with a caster level equal to your Hit Dice. Lastly, you gain a +2 bonus on weapon and spell damage rolls against chaotic creatures. This is an alignment-based effect.
Residual
: If you have this feat but you are no longer lawful neutral, you continue to gain the +2 bonus on weapon and spell damage rolls against chaotic creatures.
