Improved Forceful Charge
Source
Adventurer's Guide pg. 16
Enemies fall before this animal’s charge.
Prerequisites
:
Forceful Charge
,
Improved Bull Rush
,
Power Attack
, animal companion.
Benefit
: This animal can bull rush a target that is up to two size categories larger than itself. If it pushes the target at least 10 feet as a result of this bull rush, it can attempt a trip combat maneuver check against the target as a free action. This trip combat maneuver provokes attacks of opportunity as normal.
