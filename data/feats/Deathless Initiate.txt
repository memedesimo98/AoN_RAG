Words of Power
Deathless Initiate (Combat)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 95
For you, impending death is a call to wrath.
Prerequisites
: Str 13, Con 13, orc or half-orc,
Diehard
,
Endurance
, base attack bonus +6.
Benefit
:  You are not staggered while using the Diehard feat, but if you take a move and a standard action or a full-round action while you are at 0 or fewer hit points you take 1 point of damage. Further, while using the Diehard feat, you gain a +2 bonus on melee attacks and damage rolls.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 117
When you are at 0 or fewer hit points, you can spend 2 stamina points to avoid taking damage when you take both a move and a standard action or a full-round action.
