Cloak of Feathers
Source
Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 53
You have learned to blend in with wingless humanoids, and as long as you are not flying, you can fold your wings in a manner that resembles a cloak or robe.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, strix.
Benefit
:  When you fold your wings, you gain a +2 bonus on Disguise checks to pose as a human, and ignore the penalties for disguising yourself as a different race while doing so. Additionally, you can hide one small item within your wings’ folds (such as a potion, a wand, a weapon weighing 1 pound or less, or a similarly sized item at your GM’s discretion). Any such hidden item requires a successful DC 25 Perception check for others to notice. You can retrieve this item with a swift action, but unfolding your wings causes it to drop to the ground.
Special
: Other creatures with natural wings can also take Cloak of Feathers at the GM's discretion, ignoring the racial prerequisite
