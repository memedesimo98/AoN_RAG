Words of Power
Stone-Handed (Combat)
Source
Elemental Master's Handbook pg. 10
Drawing upon techniques to combat animated creatures, you bring a strike like an avalanche of stone.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Stunning Fist.
Benefit
: Before making a melee attack with an unarmed strike, you can expend one use of Stunning Fist to declare a stone-handed strike. Resolve the attack normally, except your attack ignores an amount of the target’s hardness equal to your monk level or one-quarter your base attack bonus (minimum 1), whichever is higher. This effect replaces other effects or conditions that would be caused by your Stunning Fist.
