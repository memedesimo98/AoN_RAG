Onslaught (Combat)
Source
Advanced Class Origins pg. 27
No one is prepared for how hard you strike until they see it firsthand.
Prerequisites
: Str 13,
Power Attack
, base attack bonus +1, sneak attack class feature.
Benefit
:  If you use Power Attack with the first melee attack you make during a surprise round, you can sneak attack even if you are not flanking your target and it’s not denied its Dexterity bonus to AC.
