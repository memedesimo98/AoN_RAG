Nosoi's Spiritsense
Source
Pathfinder #139: The Dead Road pg. 71
Even the lowliest psychopomp can sniff out the undead. A whippoorwill in a papier-mâché mask taught you how to do it.
Prerequisites
:
Duskwalker
.
Benefit
: You can cast
detect undead
as a spell-like ability at will. Your caster level for the purpose of this effect is equal to your character level.
