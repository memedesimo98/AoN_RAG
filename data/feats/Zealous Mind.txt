Words of Power
Zealous Mind
Source
Horror Adventures pg. 95
You are so steadfast in the principles of your alignment, even magic has difficult swaying you.
Prerequisites
:
Iron Will
,
Unimpeachable Honor
UI
, lawful alignment.
Benefit
: Whenever you are under the effect of a charm or compulsion effect cast by or coming from a source with a chaotic alignment or an alignment on the good and evil axis opposite of your alignment (evil if you are good, and good if you are evil) you gain a +2 on any saving throw or opposed Charisma check to break free from the effect. This bonus stacks with itself and with the bonus granted by Unimpeachable Honor.
