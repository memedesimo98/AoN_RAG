Improved Flexible Wizardry
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 150
You possess an impressive flexibility with casting that other wizards lack.
Prerequisites
:
Flexible Wizardry
,
Spell Mastery
, wizard level 8th.
Benefit
:  When you use Flexible Wizardry, you can flexibly prepare a number of spell slots equal to your Intelligence modifier + 4. Furthermore, you can finalize one of these slots with a standard action instead of a fullround action.
