Extreme Prejudice (Combat)
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 147
Your hatred of a particular type of creature has honed your killer instinct.
Prerequisites
:
Seething Hatred
, sneak attack +3d6, studied target class feature.
Benefit
:  When you succeed at a sneak attack against a creature you selected as the target of your Seething Hatred feat, you use d8s to roll sneak attack damage instead of d6s.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 119
When you succeed at a sneak attack against a creature that is not the target of your Seething Hatred
ACG
feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to treat the creature as if it were the target of your Seething Hatred for that attack.
