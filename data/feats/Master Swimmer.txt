Master Swimmer
Source
Aquatic Adventures pg. 58
You’ve trained at swimming in combat and over long distances for so long that you can move through the water like a native.
Prerequisites
:
Skill Focus (Swim)
, Swim 10 ranks.
Benefit
: You gain a swim speed of 30 feet or your unmodified base land speed, whichever is slower; your racial bonus on Swim checks for having a swim speed is +2, instead of +8.
