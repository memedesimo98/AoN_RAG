Additional Affiliations
Source
Adventurer's Guide pg. 6
You have an expansive personality and can forge more affiliations than normal.
Benefit
: Increase your affiliation slot total by 2.
Normal
: You have a number of affiliation slots equal to your Charisma modifier + 1 (minimum 1).
Special
: You can take this feat multiple times; each time you do so, your number of affiliation slots increases by 2.
