Courage in Numbers (Teamwork)
Source
Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 21
Your allies give you courage when you face an enemy as one.
Prerequisites
:
Iron Will.
Benefit
:  You gain a +2 morale bonus on saves against
fear for every ally within 10 feet who possesses this feat
(maximum +8).
