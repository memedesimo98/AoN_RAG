Surprise Strike (Combat)
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 67
You actually seem to do more damage when frantically trying to avoid your enemies.
Prerequisites
:
Cautious Fighter
,
Desperate Swing
, base attack bonus +6, halfling.
Benefit
:  Once per day, when fighting defensively or making an attack of opportunity while taking the total defense action with the Desperate Swing feat, you take no penalty on the attack roll.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133
After you have used this feat once, you can spend 5 stamina points to use it additional times per day, at a cost of 5 stamina points per use.
