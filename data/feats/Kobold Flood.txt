Words of Power
Kobold Flood (Combat)
Source
Kobolds of Golarion pg. 25
You are an expert at keeping creatures on the ground.
Prerequisites
:
Combat Expertise
,
Kobold Groundling
,
Kobold Style
, Small size or smaller.
Benefit
:  You can make a grapple combat maneuver check
to mount a prone Medium or Large foe. If you’re successful,
you move into the target’s square and are considered to be
on top of the target. For the target to stand, it must succeed
at a combat maneuver check to break your grapple.
