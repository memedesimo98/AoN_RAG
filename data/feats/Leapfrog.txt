Leapfrog (Combat, Performance)
Source
People of the River pg. 15
You can use an opponent as a springboard.
Prerequisites
: Acrobatics 3 ranks.
Benefit
:  When you spend a swift action to attempt a performance combat check after a successful charge attack or combat maneuver, you gain a +2 bonus on your performance combat check and gain a +4 bonus on your next Acrobatics, Climb, Fly, Ride, or Swim check attempted before the end of your next turn.
