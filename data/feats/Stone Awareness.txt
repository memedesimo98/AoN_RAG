Stone Awareness
Source
Giants Revisited pg. 49
You can sense creatures touching earth or stone.
Prerequisites
: Wis 13, Perception 10 ranks, stone giant.
Benefit
:  You gain tremorsense 15 feet when in contact with earth or stone, including worked stone, and gain all the benefits of the tremorsense universal monster ability.
