Words of Power
Target of Opportunity (Combat, Teamwork)
Source
Ultimate Combat pg. 122
You and your allies pelt your enemies with a deadly barrage of missiles.
Prerequisites
:
Point-Blank Shot
, base attack bonus +6.
Benefit
:  When an ally who also has this feat makes a ranged attack and hits an opponent within 30 feet of you, you can spend an immediate action to make a single ranged attack against that opponent. Your ranged weapon must be in hand, loaded, and ready to be fired or thrown for you to make the ranged attack.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133
You can spend 5 stamina points to make the attack granted by this feat as an attack of opportunity instead of an immediate action. You can use this combat trick only once per round.
