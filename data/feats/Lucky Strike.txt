Lucky Strike (Combat)
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 67
Your luck increases the potency of your weapon attacks.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +5, adaptive luck racial trait, halfling.
Benefit
:  Spend a use of your adaptive luck racial trait to reroll the damage from a single weapon attack. You deal damage equal to the new damage roll, or the original roll, whichever is greater.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125
You can spend 5 stamina points instead of a use of adaptable luck
ARG
to reroll the damage from a single attack.
