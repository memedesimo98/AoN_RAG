Strangler (MC)
Source
Monster Codex pg. 20
You can choke foes with a lasso.
Prerequisites
:
Exotic Weapon Proficiency (lasso)
,
Weapon Focus (lasso).
Benefit
:  When you successfully entangle a creature with a lasso (
Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player’s Guide
178) and beat the target’s CMD by 5 or more, the target cannot speak above a whisper and must succeed at a concentration check (DC = 20 + your CMB + the spell level) to cast a spell with a verbal component, use a command word item, or use any magic requiring speech. Additionally, the DC of an Escape Artist check to slip free of a lasso you are using equals 15 or your CMD, whichever is higher.
