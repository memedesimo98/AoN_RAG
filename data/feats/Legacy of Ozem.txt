Legacy of Ozem
Source
Champions of Purity pg. 23
Your purity is so great that the blades and bows of long-forgotten
heroes sing to you as if they had been made for
your righteous hands.
Prerequisites
: Base attack bonus +5, good alignment.
Benefit
:  When using good-aligned weapons, including
those under the effects of a
bless weapon
spell, you gain a
+1 sacred bonus on damage rolls.
