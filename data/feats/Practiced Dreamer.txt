Words of Power
Practiced Dreamer
Source
Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 30
Your time in the Dimension of Dreams has improved your ability to manipulate its landscape.
Prerequisites
: Cha 15,
Lucid Dreamer
, Knowledge (planes) 5 ranks.
Benefit
: Once per day, you can reroll a failed attempt to perform an impossible action, such as casting a spell you can’t normally cast, gaining an effect of a spell as if it had been cast, or conjuring a magic item. You also gain an additional +2 bonus on Charisma checks to determine your initial condition when entering a dream with your lucid body.
