Improved Hammer Throw (Combat)
Source
Heroes from the Fringe pg. 9
Your ability to knock down your foes with ranged attacks is impressive.
Prerequisites
: Int 13,
Combat Expertise
,
Hammer Throw
,
Improved Trip
,
Point-Blank Shot
,
Precise Shot
, base attack bonus +6, dwarf.
Benefit
: You can use the Hammer Throw feat against targets at any range and of any size.
