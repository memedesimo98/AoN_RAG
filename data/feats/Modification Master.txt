Words of Power
Modification Master (Combat)
Source
Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 11
You can create inconceivable bastardizations of the smith’s art.
Prerequisites
:
Creative Armorsmith
,
Armor Adept
, and Craft (armor) 7 ranks, or Creative Weaponsmith,
Weapon Adept
, and Craft (weapons) 7 ranks; Knowledge (engineering) 7 ranks.
Benefit
: You can use Creative Armorsmith or Creative Weaponsmith to add a modification to a weapon or suit of armor that already has a modification. The modification you are adding must be one that you have selected with Armor Adept or Weapon Adept. In addition, others can use armor or weapons with your temporary modifications.
