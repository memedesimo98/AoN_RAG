Mother's Gift
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 185
You inherit a special boon from your hag parent.
Prerequisites
: Changeling.
Benefit
:  Your dark legacy manifests in one of the following ways. You choose the manifestation when you choose the feat, and once selected it cannot be changed.
Hag Claws (Ex)
: You gain a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with your claws.
Surprisingly Tough (Ex)
: Your natural armor bonus increases by +1.
Uncanny Resistance (Su)
: You gain spell resistance equal to 6 + your character level.
Special
:  You can gain this feat up to three times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take the feat, you must select a different manifestation.
