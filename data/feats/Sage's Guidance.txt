Sage's Guidance (Familiar)
Source
Familiar Folio pg. 19
Your familiar’s keen observations help you defeat foes. Prerequisite: Must have a familiar with the sage archetype (see page 11).
Prerequisites
: Must have a familiar with the sage archetype (see page 11).
Benefit
:  When your familiar succeeds at a Knowledge check to identify a hazard or an opponent’s weaknesses and abilities, you gain a +2 insight bonus on attacks and skill checks against the opponent or hazard for 1 round. If your familiar is sharing a space with the opponent or hazard, this bonus is granted to all allies who can see and understand the familiar.
