Murderous Sniper (Combat)
Source
Chronicle of Legends pg. 9
Your study of death allows you to pinpoint weaknesses from afar.
Prerequisites
:
Death attack class feature.
Benefit
: You can make a death attack using a ranged weapon. Your target must be within 30 feet when attempting the death attack. For every additional round you spend studying the target, you can extend the range from which you can make a ranged death attack by 10 feet.
