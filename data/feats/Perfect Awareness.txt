Perfect Awareness (Combat, Meditation)
Source
Faiths and Philosophies pg. 19
You can set aside all irrelevant sensory information to understand your surroundings perfectly for a moment.
Prerequisites
: Wis 17,
Combat Meditation
,
Meditation Master
, character level 9th.
Benefit
:  Once per day, you can take 20 on a Perception check as a move action.
