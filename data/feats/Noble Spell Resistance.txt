Noble Spell Resistance
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 105
Your ascension is complete; you have the spell resistance approaching that of a demon.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, Wis 13,
Greater Drow Nobility
, character level 13th, drow.
Benefit
:  Your spell resistance is equal to 11 + your character level.
Special
:  You receive a +1 circumstance bonus on Diplomacy and Intimidate checks made against any drow.
