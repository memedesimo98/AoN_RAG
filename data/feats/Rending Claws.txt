Words of Power
Rending Claws (Combat)
Source
Advanced Player's Guide pg. 168
Your claw attacks do greater harm to your enemy.
Prerequisites
: Str 13, two claw natural weapon attacks, base attack bonus +6.
Benefit
:  If you hit a creature with two claw attacks in the same turn, the second claw attack deals an additional 1d6 points of damage. This damage is precision damage and is not multiplied on a critical hit. You can use this feat once per round.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130
When you make two claw attacks but hit with only one, you can spend 5 stamina points to deal the precision damage granted by this feat anyway.
