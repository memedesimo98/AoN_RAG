Alien Mindpaths (Teamwork)
Source
Inner Sea Races pg. 201
When you’re in the company of other aliens, your thoughts become difficult to follow.
Prerequisites
: Android, kasatha, lashunta, or Triaxian.
Benefit
:  Whenever you’re adjacent to at least one other android, kasatha, lashunta, or Triaxian ally who also has this feat, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus on saves against mind-affecting effects and divination (scrying) effects, unless the effect comes from an android, kasatha, lashunta, or Triaxian.
