Create Reliquary Arms and Shields
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 148
Your magical creations are infused with divine power.
Prerequisites
:
Craft Magic Arms and Armor
, ability to cast consecrate or desecrate.
Benefit
:  When you craft a magic weapon, magic armor, or magic shield, you may add one casting of
consecrate
or
desecrate
as part of the item crafting process. This increases the item’s price by 250 gp. The item becomes a reliquary and can be used as a holy (or unholy) symbol divine focus of your deity. If you cast
consecrate
or
desecrate
, your reliquary counts as a permanent fixture for that spell while it remains in the spell’s area.
