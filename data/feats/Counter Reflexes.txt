Words of Power
Counter Reflexes (Combat)
Source
Advanced Class Guide pg. 144
Your opponent’s advanced training in moving around you is surpassed only by your readiness for it.
Prerequisites
:
Anticipate Dodge
,
Dodge
, Mobility; base attack bonus +9, brawler level 6th, or monk level 6th.
Benefit
:  Opponents with the Mobility feat do not gain the dodge bonus granted by that feat when they provoke attacks of opportunity by moving out of or within your threatened area.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 116
When an opponent with the Mobility feat moves out of or within your threatened area, you can spend 2 stamina points to gain a +4 bonus on your attack of opportunity.
