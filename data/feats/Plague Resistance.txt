Plague Resistance
Source
Villain Codex pg. 68
Diseases tend to have milder effects on you.
Prerequisites
: Constitution 13.
Benefit
: When you take damage or drain to your ability scores as the result of a disease, roll damage and drain to each ability score twice and take the lower result. You suffer any other effects of the disease as normal.
