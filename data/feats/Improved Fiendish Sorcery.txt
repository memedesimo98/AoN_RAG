Improved Fiendish Sorcery
Source
Blood of Fiends pg. 25
Your skill with foul sorcery is even more potent than that of others of your race.
Prerequisites
: Fiendish sorcery racial trait, tiefling.
Benefit
:  If you are a sorcerer and possess the Rakshasa bloodline (
Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic
68) or Shadow bloodline (
Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player’s
Guide 140), treat your Charisma score as 2 points higher for all sorcerer class abilities.
