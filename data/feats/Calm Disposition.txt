Calm Disposition (Faction)
Source
Faction Guide pg. 56
You always appear to be in control of your situation.
Prerequisites
: Prophets of Kalistrade 10 TPA.
Benefit
: You gain +2 bonus on saving throws against effects that affect your composure. This includes all mind-affecting spells, as well as any spell or ability that would cause you to behave in an out-of-control manner, such as fear effects and nausea. This does not include physical effects such as fatigue, and does not reduce any damage taken from concurrent effects.
