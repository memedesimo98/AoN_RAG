Intimidating Gaze
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 153
There is something in your eyes that scares people.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13+, stern gaze class feature, Intimidate 5 ranks.
Benefit
:  Once per day, as a free action, when making an Intimidate skill check, you can roll two dice and take the higher result.
