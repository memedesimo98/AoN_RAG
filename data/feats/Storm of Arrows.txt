Storm of Arrows (Combat)
Source
Chronicle of Legends pg. 9
You can rain arrows upon a greater number of foes.
Prerequisites
:
Hail of arrows class feature.
Benefit
: When using hail of arrows, you can target up to two targets for every arcane archer level you have.
