Words of Power
Blight Guide
Source
Pathfinder #119: Prisoners of the Blight pg. 61
Your confidence in exploring dangerous regions inspires the same in your companions.
Prerequisites
:
Blight Survivalist
,
Skill Focus (Survival).
Benefit
:  Your bonus on saving throws gained from the Blight Survivalist feat extends to your allies within 30 feet. Furthermore, once per day, if you would fail a saving throw against an environmental hazard or weather effect, you can reroll that save, but you must use the second result, even if it is lower.
