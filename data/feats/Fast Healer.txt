Fast Healer
Source
Advanced Player's Guide pg. 160
You benefit greatly from your healing, be it from spells or natural healing.
Prerequisites
: Con 13,
Diehard
,
Endurance.
Benefit
:  When you regain hit points by resting or through magical healing, you recover additional hit points equal to half your Constitution modifier (minimum +1).
