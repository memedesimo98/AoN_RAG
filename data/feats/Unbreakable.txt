Unbreakable (Combat)
Source
Adventurer's Guide pg. 86
As a veteran Gray Maiden, you can endure a staggering amount of punishment.
Prerequisites
: Con 13,
Endurance
,
Toughness
, fighter level 4th.
Benefit
: Whenever you gain 1 hit point as a fighter favored class benefit, you gain an additional hit point. Treat your effective Constitution score as being 4 higher for the purpose of determining when you would die from hit point damage. These benefits are retroactive.
