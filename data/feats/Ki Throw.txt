Words of Power
Ki Throw (Combat)
Source
Advanced Player's Guide pg. 164
Your physical control and mastery of momentum allows you to throw enemies.
Prerequisites
:
Improved Trip
,
Improved Unarmed Strike.
Benefit
:  On a successful unarmed trip attack against a target your size or smaller, you may throw the target prone in any square you threaten rather than its own square. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity, and you cannot throw the creature into a space occupied by other creatures.
Special
:  A monk may gain
Ki
Throw as a bonus feat at 10th level. A monk with this feat can affect creatures larger than his own size by spending 1
ki
point per size category difference.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125
You can spend 5 stamina points to throw a creature one size category larger than you.
