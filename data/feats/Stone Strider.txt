Words of Power
Stone Strider
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 216
You discern the best path through rocky terrain.
Prerequisites
: Stonecunning racial trait, worshiper of Torag.
Benefit
:  You can move through any sort of nonmagical rocky terrain without taking damage, becoming slowed due to difficult terrain, or suffering any other impairments the terrain would otherwise cause.
