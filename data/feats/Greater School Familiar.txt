Greater School Familiar
Source
Familiar Folio pg. 14
Your school familiar’s powers are greater than others of its kind.
Prerequisites
:
School Familiar
, wizard level 10th, specialized arcane school class feature.
Benefit
:  Your school familiar gains the greater school power from the school familiar archetype.
