Dark Adept
Source
Pathfinder #18: Descent into Midnight pg. 14
,
Pathfinder #14: Children of the Void pg. 46
,
Pathfinder #15: The Armageddon Echo pg. 59
You gain several additional spell-like abilities from your dark elven heritage.
Prerequisites
: Drow, character level 3rd
Benefit
: You gain three new spell-like abilities, each usable once per day. These spell-like abilities are
detect magic
,
feather fall
, and
levitate
. Your caster level for these spell-like abilities equals your total character level.
