Words of Power
Sin Seer
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 155
Unlike others who have taken the oath against undeath (see page 60), your preoccupation with the undead does not cloud your view of good and evil.
Prerequisites
: Detect undead paladin class feature.
Benefit
:  You gain the
detect evil
class feature. You may use it or the
detect undead
class feature, but not at the same time.
