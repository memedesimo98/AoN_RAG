Poisoner's Channel
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 215
You cause anyone damaged by your negative energy to become susceptible to poison.
Prerequisites
: Channel energy 3d6, worshiper of Norgorber.
Benefit
:  Once per day when you channel negative energy to damage living creatures, you can cause any creatures that failed their save against your channel to take a –4 penalty on saving throws against poison effects. This penalty lasts for 1 minute.
