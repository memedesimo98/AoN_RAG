Words of Power
Skeleton Summoner
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 155
The walking dead respond to your call.
Prerequisites
:
Spell Focus (necromancy)
, ability to cast
summon monster
.
Benefit
:  Add “human skeleton” to the list of creatures you can summon with
summon monster I
and “human skeletal champion” to the list of creatures you can summon with
summon monster III
.
Once per day, when you cast
summon monster
, you may summon a skeletal version of one of the creatures on that spell’s summoning list (apply the skeleton template to that creature to create this monster).
