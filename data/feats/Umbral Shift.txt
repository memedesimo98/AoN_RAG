Umbral Shift (Teamwork)
Source
Inner Sea Races pg. 209
Your shadows dance in the darkness, carrying you with them swiftly and silently so that you’re able to swap places with your kindred.
Prerequisites
:
Darkness
racial spell-like ability.
Benefit
:  Whenever you and an ally with the
darkness
racial spell-like ability who also has this feat are each in an area of darkness within 20 feet of an object radiating darkness from a
darkness
spell, you can swap places with that ally as a swift action. Your ally must be willing and must spend an immediate action to complete the swap.
