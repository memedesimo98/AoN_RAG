Shifter's Rush
Source
Ultimate Wilderness pg. 117
You can use you wild shape ability as you are moving toward your enemy.
Prerequisites
: Wild shape class feature, shifter level 4th
Benefit
: When you use a move action to move 10 feet or more or when you charge, you can use wild shape as a free action during that movement.
