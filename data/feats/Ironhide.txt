Words of Power
Ironhide
Source
Advanced Player's Guide pg. 164
Your skin is thicker and more resilient than that of most of your people.
Prerequisites
: Con 13; dwarf, half-orc, or orc.
Benefit
:  You gain a +1 natural armor bonus due to your unusually tough hide.
