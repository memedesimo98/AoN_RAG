Wyvaran Spellcasting
Source
Legacy of Dragons pg. 19
Your unique physiology makes you particularly adept at wielding draconic magic.
Prerequisites
: Knowledge (arcana) 6 ranks, wyvaran.
Benefit
: Select one spell you can cast that you gained through your draconic bloodline, the Scalykind domain (
Pathfinder Campaign Setting: The Inner Sea World Guide 216
), or the Dragon subdomain (
Pathfinder Campaign Setting: Inner Sea Gods 224
). It must be at least 1 spell level lower than the highest-level spell you can cast. You can cast this spell as a spell-like ability twice per day.
