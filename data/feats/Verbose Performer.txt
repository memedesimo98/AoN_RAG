Verbose Performer (Faction)
Source
Faction Guide pg. 58
The range of your bardic performances increases.
Prerequisites
: Bardic performance class feature,
Extra Performance
,
Master Performer
, bard level 8th, Kitharodian Academy 15 TPA.
Benefit
: Double the range of any bardic performance ability that possesses a range in the ability description.
