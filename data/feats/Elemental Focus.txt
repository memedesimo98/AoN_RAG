Words of Power
Elemental Focus
Source
Advanced Player's Guide pg. 158
Your spells of a certain element are more difficult to resist.
Benefit
:  Choose one energy type (acid, cold, electricity, or fire). Add +1 to the Difficulty Class for all saving throws against spells that deal damage of the energy type you select.
Special
:  You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take this feat, it applies to a new energy type.
Mythic Elemental Focus
Source
Mythic Adventures pg. 64
Your elemental spells are potent and effective.
Prerequisites
:
Elemental Focus
APG
.
Benefit
:  Choose an energy type in which you already have Elemental Focus. The increase to save DC provided by Elemental Focus and Greater Elemental Focus for spells of the selected energy type is 1 higher. When casting a spell with this energy type’s descriptor, you can expend one use of mythic power as part of the casting to force the spell’s targets to roll their saving throws twice, taking the lower result.
