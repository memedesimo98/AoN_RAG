Consumption (Blood Hex)
Source
Magic Tactics Toolbox pg. 29
You cause your rivals to spend additional precious resources while wounded.
Prerequisites
: Int 13, Use Magic Device 5 ranks.
Benefit
: As a full-round action, you can curse a creature you’ve dealt damage to since the beginning of your last round to use certain powers quickly. The creature must succeed at a Fortitude save or if it uses any of the following class abilities during the next minute, it must spend at least two daily uses or rounds of that ability: arcane pool
