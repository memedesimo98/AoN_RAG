Tantrum
Source
Gnomes of Golarion pg. 31
Your crying, sputtering, and even frothing at the mouth makes you so laughably incoherent that opponents are apt to dismiss you as a threat.
Prerequisites
: Bluff 1 rank, rage class feature, gnome.
Benefit
: You may use the Bluff skill to feint while raging. The effect of the feint lasts only until the first time you hit the target.
