Improvised Defenses (Combat)
Source
Martial Arts Handbook pg. 9
You can deftly defend yourself with makeshift weapons.
Prerequisites
:
Catch Off-Guard
or
Throw Anything
, base attack bonus +1.
Benefit
: You gain a +1 shield bonus to AC when you are wielding an improvised weapon and have your other hand free.
