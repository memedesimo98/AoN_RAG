Vigilant Eidolon
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 159
Your eidolon is highly observant, and its link with you increases your own watchfulness.
Prerequisites
: Eidolon class feature.
Benefit
:  While your eidolon is within your reach, you gain a +4 bonus on Perception checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in Perception, this bonus increases to +8. This does not apply if your eidolon is helpless or unconscious.
