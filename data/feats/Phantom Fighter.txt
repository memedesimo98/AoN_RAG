Words of Power
Phantom Fighter
Source
Occult Adventures pg. 137
Your phantom is a deadly foe of incorporeal adversaries.
Prerequisites
: Phantom class feature, phantom with the magic attacks ability.
Benefit
:  Your phantom’s natural weapons are treated as having the
ghost touch
property. In addition, when you cast a touch spell to be delivered by your phantom, you can modify the spell as if you possessed the Ectoplasmic Spell
APG
metamagic feat.
