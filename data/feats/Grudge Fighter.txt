Words of Power
Grudge Fighter (Combat)
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 141
You feel great anger at anyone who dares to attack you, and this fury makes your own attacks that much stronger.
Prerequisites
: Orc.
Benefit
:  You gain a +1 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls made against any creature that attacked you in the current combat.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122
When you hit a creature that attacked you in the current combat, you can spend up to 2 stamina points to deal an amount of extra damage to it equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
