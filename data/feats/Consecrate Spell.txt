Consecrate Spell (Metamagic)
Source
Blood of Angels pg. 25
You infuse spells with heavenly power, drastically increasing their effectiveness against would-be heralds of evil.
Prerequisites
: Aasimar, able to prepare or cast
consecrate
.
Benefit
:  A consecrated spell is treated as a maximized spell against evil creatures and creatures with the evil subtype. Against all other creatures, this feat does not modify the spell in any way (for example, a consecrated
fireball
acts as a normal
fireball
against neutral or good creatures). This feat does not stack with Maximize Spell. A consecrated spell uses up a spell slot two levels higher than the spell’s actual level.
