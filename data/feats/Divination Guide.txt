Words of Power
Divination Guide
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 210
,
Faiths of Purity pg. 24
You deity grants you greater guidance and divine insight.
Prerequisites
: Caster level 3rd, ability to cast
augury
or
divination
, must worship a deity.
Benefit
:  Whenever you cast
augury
or
divination
, you receive further guidance from your deity’s agents. You gain a +1 sacred bonus if your worship a good deity or a +1 profane bonus if you worship an evil deity (if you worship a neutral deity the type of bonus is either determined by the type of energy you channel with the channel energy class feature or, if you don’t have that class ability, your choice upon taking this feat) on all saving throws for 1 minute per caster level of the
augury
or
divination
spell you cast.
