Conditional Spell (Metamagic)
Source
Healer's Handbook pg. 8
You know how to apply restrictions on how a creature can use the benefits of your spells.
Benefit
: A conditional spell is subject to the rules laid out in the spell
conditional favor
(
Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Intrigue 206
). A conditional spell takes up a spell slot 1 level higher than normal.
