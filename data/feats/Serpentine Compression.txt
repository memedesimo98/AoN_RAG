Serpentine Compression
Source
Monster Codex pg. 200
You can squeeze through small areas thanks to your slippery scales and lithe musculature.
Prerequisites
: Serpentfolk.
Benefit
:  You gain the compression universal monster ability (
Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 2
295), allowing you to move through an area as small as one-quarter your space without squeezing, or one-eighth your space when squeezing.
