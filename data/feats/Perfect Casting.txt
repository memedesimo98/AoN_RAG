Words of Power
Perfect Casting
Source
Inner Sea Gods pg. 215
You unlock greater secrets of divine spellcasting from the First Vault.
Prerequisites
:
Combat Casting
,
Divine Dignity
, ability to cast divine spells, worshiper of Abadar.
Benefit
:  When you use Divine Dignity, it can affect the casting of any of your divine spells, not just harmless and domain spells.
