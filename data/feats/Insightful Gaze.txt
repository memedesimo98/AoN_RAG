Words of Power
Insightful Gaze
Source
Ultimate Magic pg. 153
In your personal interactions, you notice what others don’t. It’s hard to get anything past you.
Prerequisites
: Stern gaze class feature, Sense Motive 5 ranks.
Benefit
:  Whenever you make a Sense Motive check to oppose someone’s Bluff check, you can roll two dice and take the higher result.
