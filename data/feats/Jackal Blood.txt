Jackal Blood
Source
Dark Markets - A Guide to Katapesh pg. 13
A jackalwere crouches somewhere in your family tree.
Benefit
: You gain a +4 bonus on saving throws to resist spells or effects that would cause you to fall asleep. You also gain a +2 bonus on Listen checks.
