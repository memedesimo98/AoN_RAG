Hydraulic Maneuver
Source
Advanced Race Guide pg. 177
You can use your
hydraulic push
to disarm or trip.
Prerequisites
:
Hydraulic push
spell-like ability, undine.
Benefit
:  You may use
hydraulic push
to attempt a bull rush, disarm, dirty trick (blind or dazzle, see ), or trip combat maneuver. Each time you use
hydraulic push
, you must decide which of the allowed combat maneuvers you want to perform. You may use this feat with your
hydraulic push
racial spell-like ability, your class-granted use of
hydraulic push
, or any
hydraulic push
spells you cast, but not with magic items or other external sources that use that spell.
Normal
:
Hydraulic push
can only be used to make a bull rush combat maneuver.
