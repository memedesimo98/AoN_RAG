Fighting Frenzy (Combat, Teamwork)
Source
Advanced Class Origins pg. 27
You feed off the rage of your allies.
Prerequisites
: Cha 13, rage class feature.
Benefit
:  When you are in a rage and an ally with this feat within 60 feet is in a rage, you don’t take the normal –2 penalty to AC for being in a rage. If you would take a larger penalty to AC for being in a rage, reduce that penalty by 2.
