Sickening Critical (Combat, Critical)
Source
PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 133
Your critical hits cause opponents to become sickened.
Prerequisites
:
Critical Focus
, base attack bonus +11.
Benefit
: Whenever you score a critical hit, your opponent becomes sickened for 1 minute. The effects of this feat do not stack. Additional hits instead add to the effect's duration.
Special
: You can only apply the effects of one critical feat to a given critical hit unless you possess Critical Mastery.
Combat Trick
(from the
Combat Stamina
feat)
Source
Pathfinder Unchained pg. 131
When you threaten a critical hit using this feat, before rolling the confirmation roll, you can spend 5 stamina points. If you do and the critical hit is confirmed, the foe is nauseated for 1d4+1 rounds unless it succeeds at a Fortitude save with a DC equal to 10 + your base attack bonus, which reduces the effect to the normal sickened condition.
